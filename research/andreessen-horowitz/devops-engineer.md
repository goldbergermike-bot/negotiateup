#### American Dynamism — The $15B Intelligence Cost Collapse Premium

DevOps Engineers at a16z are essential to the intelligence cost collapse because they control the infrastructure costs that determine whether AI-native systems are economically viable at scale.

- **Infrastructure Cost Collapse:** The intelligence cost collapse thesis has a literal infrastructure dimension — AI compute costs, data storage costs, and pipeline processing costs must decline for the thesis to work. The DevOps Engineer who optimizes cloud spend by 40% while increasing throughput by 3x is directly proving the thesis. This measurable impact commands compensation premium.
- **$15B Fund Reliability:** When infrastructure goes down at a16z, it doesn't just affect a product — it affects investment operations for a $15B fund. A missed deal alert, a delayed portfolio report, a failed LP dashboard refresh — these have real financial consequences measured in millions. The reliability guarantee that a DevOps Engineer provides is directly tied to fund performance.
- **AI Infrastructure Scaling:** The $15B January 2026 fundraise is accelerating a16z's adoption of AI-native tools — LLM-powered deal analysis, automated portfolio monitoring, AI-driven market intelligence. The DevOps Engineer builds and maintains the GPU infrastructure, model serving pipelines, and data processing systems that power these AI capabilities.
- **Security as American Dynamism:** The American Dynamism portfolio includes defense tech and national security companies. The DevOps Engineer must maintain infrastructure that meets government-adjacent security standards — FedRAMP awareness, SOC 2 compliance, zero-trust networking — adding complexity and value to the role.
