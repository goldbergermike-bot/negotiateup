**Negotiation DNA**

Security Engineers at PayPal protect a platform that processes billions of financial transactions across 400M+ active accounts, making this one of the highest-stakes security engineering roles in the industry. The February 2026 Cymbio acquisition introduced new attack surfaces: Cymbio's commerce infrastructure must be audited, hardened, and integrated into PayPal's security posture without creating vulnerabilities in the branded checkout pipeline. Security Engineers are conducting threat modeling for the merged platform, implementing security controls across Cymbio's APIs, and ensuring the integrated system meets PCI-DSS, SOC 2, and financial regulatory requirements.

PayPal's 50% biometric authentication goal places Security Engineers at the absolute center of the company's identity strategy. These engineers design and implement biometric template storage, liveness detection, device attestation, and anti-spoofing systems that determine whether biometric authentication is secure enough to process financial transactions. The PYUSD stablecoin adds blockchain security requirements — smart contract auditing, private key management, and on-chain monitoring for suspicious transactions. With $30B+ in revenue and regulatory scrutiny from global financial authorities, PayPal pays a significant premium for Security Engineers who bring depth in payment security, identity verification, and blockchain security.
