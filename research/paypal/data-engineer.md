**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at PayPal build and maintain the data infrastructure that powers analytics, machine learning, and business intelligence across a platform serving 400M+ active accounts and processing $30B+ in annual revenue. The February 2026 Cymbio acquisition has created a massive data integration challenge: Cymbio's commerce data — merchant catalogs, multi-channel transaction logs, marketplace activity — must be integrated into PayPal's data lake and warehouse infrastructure to enable unified analytics for the Branded Checkout Reset. Data Engineers are building the pipelines that merge these data estates, enabling cross-platform reporting and ML model training.

PayPal's 50% biometric authentication target generates enormous volumes of authentication event data that must be captured, stored, and made available for fraud detection models and authentication optimization. Data Engineers design the streaming and batch pipelines that process biometric authentication events in real time. The PYUSD stablecoin adds on-chain data ingestion requirements — blockchain transaction data must be integrated with traditional payment data to provide unified settlement reporting. With the complexity of PayPal's data ecosystem and the urgency of the Cymbio integration, Data Engineers command competitive compensation against offers from hyperscaler data teams and fintech companies.
