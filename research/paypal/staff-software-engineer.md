These engineers lead design reviews spanning payments, identity, risk, and blockchain settlement domains. With $30B+ in revenue at stake and competitive pressure from Stripe, Block, Apple Pay, and emerging fintech players, PayPal compensates Staff Engineers at levels designed to retain talent that would otherwise be recruited by FAANG companies or well-funded startups.
