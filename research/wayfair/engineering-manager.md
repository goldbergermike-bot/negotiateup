As an Engineering Manager, you have strong leverage to capitalize on this margin gift: Wayfair's margins are temporarily protected in 2026 because tariffs on home goods imports are delayed to 2027. This margin gift means Wayfair has more comp budget in 2026 than it will in 2027+. Your argument should be: "I want to lock in a higher base salary during the 2026 Tariff Holiday — while Wayfair's margins are protected. Once tariffs hit in 2027, comp budgets will tighten. The base salary I negotiate now is my floor forever — I want to maximize it during the margin window." My base salary locked in now persists through 2027+ tariff headwinds — I'm negotiating for permanent comp during a temporary margin advantage." As the Engineering Manager who will lead teams through the tariff-driven cost restructuring in 2027, your leadership is critical — lock in maximum base while the margin window is open.
