For a Software Engineer joining now, the timing is exceptional: you are entering at the ground floor of a technology transformation that will define Shift4's next growth chapter. Your negotiating position is strengthened by the fact that Shift4 must staff up rapidly to support both the Stablecoin Settlement rollout and ongoing Skytab POS expansion across thousands of new merchant locations.

Use this to your advantage: the company is calibrating its bands upward and you should anchor toward the top of range.
