**Negotiation DNA**

As a Data Scientist at Payerve, you extract actionable intelligence from payment transaction data flowing through the platform across the MENA corridor. Payerve's Dubai Intelligent Automation focus relies on data scientists who can build fraud detection models, optimize payment routing algorithms, and generate merchant analytics that drive platform stickiness. Your models directly influence Regional Scale by improving approval rates, reducing fraud losses, and enabling data-driven market expansion decisions across UAE, Saudi Arabia, and emerging MENA markets.

The data science function at a private growth-stage payment facilitator in the Gulf is uniquely impactful. Every model you build operates on live transaction data across multiple currencies (AED, SAR, EGP, BHD) and must account for regional payment patterns that differ significantly from Western markets — higher cash-on-delivery conversion, distinct fraud vectors, and Ramadan/Eid seasonality effects on transaction volumes. Dubai's tax-free salary amplifies your base compensation, while your option grant (typically 0.08-0.15% at this stage) captures the compounding value of models that scale across Payerve's growing MENA footprint.

The role demands bridging traditional and digital payments in the Arab world through data — analyzing merchant behavior patterns, modeling the cash-to-digital transition, and building predictive systems that help Payerve's payment facilitation platform serve the evolving needs of Gulf economies. Data scientists with fintech domain expertise, Arabic-language NLP capabilities, or GCC payment data experience carry significant negotiation leverage in the Dubai market.
