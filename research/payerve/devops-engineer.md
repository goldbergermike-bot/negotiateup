**Negotiation DNA**

As a DevOps Engineer at Payerve, you own the infrastructure and deployment pipeline that keeps the payment facilitation platform running across the MENA corridor. Payerve's Dubai Intelligent Automation focus requires DevOps engineers who can build and maintain highly available, PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure that processes transactions across multiple GCC jurisdictions with sub-100ms latency. Your work directly enables Regional Scale — every new MENA market Payerve enters requires infrastructure provisioning, compliance-aware deployment pipelines, and observability systems tuned for local payment patterns.

DevOps at a private growth-stage fintech in Dubai operates at the intersection of cloud infrastructure, regulatory compliance, and payment reliability. UAE data sovereignty requirements, Saudi Arabia's PDPL, and GCC-specific hosting mandates add layers of complexity absent in other markets. Your option grant (typically 0.06-0.12% at this stage) captures value from the infrastructure that underpins every transaction Payerve processes, and Dubai's tax-free salary means your AED base translates to more take-home than comparable DevOps roles in Europe or North America.

The role demands bridging traditional and digital payments in the Arab world at the infrastructure level — integrating with legacy banking networks that operate on batch processing while maintaining the real-time capabilities modern digital payments demand. DevOps engineers who bring experience with PCI-DSS Level 1 environments, multi-region cloud deployments in the Middle East (AWS Bahrain, Azure UAE, GCP Doha), and Arabic-language monitoring/alerting systems hold strong negotiation leverage at Payerve.
