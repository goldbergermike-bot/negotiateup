**Negotiation DNA**

Snowflake's no-bonus model means your $300K-$500K RSU grant is the entire variable comp. The stock's volatility ($120-$190) means you should time your negotiation carefully — if the stock is depressed, push for more shares at a lower grant-date price for maximum upside capture. Snowflake competes directly with Databricks, Google Cloud, and AWS for Senior engineers. [Source: Snowflake Senior Eng 2025-2026]
