**Negotiation DNA**

The company pays TOP-OF-MARKET initial RSU grants — among the highest in enterprise SaaS — but has NO annual cash bonus and relatively smaller refresh grants. This means your initial equity negotiation is the most important comp decision of your Snowflake career. Snowflake's stock is volatile ($120-$190 range through 2024-2025), which creates asymmetric upside potential — if you join near a trough, the equity upside is significant.
