Every role at Perplexity — from engineering to design to product — is building the infrastructure of this paradigm shift. As a Staff Software Engineer, you are the architect of the systems that make AEO possible at scale — the retrieval pipelines, the ranking algorithms, the inference infrastructure, and the answer quality frameworks that define what it means to deliver a trustworthy AI answer. When negotiating, frame your value as: "I'm not joining a startup. I'm building the platform that replaces Google Search for the AI era. AEO is the new SEO, and Perplexity is defining it. My equity should reflect that I'm an architect of the most important shift in information access since 1998."
