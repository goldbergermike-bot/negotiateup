### Identity Verification Platform Lever

Plaid's identity verification product is the company's fastest-growing revenue line, processing KYC/AML checks across banking, crypto, and lending. Engineers working on identity systems touch real-time document verification, biometric matching, and fraud-signal aggregation — all under strict regulatory timelines. Frame any experience with identity, fraud, or compliance systems as directly accelerating Plaid's #1 growth bet.

If your background includes document OCR, liveness detection, or PII-handling infrastructure, you carry a scarcity premium. Plaid competes with Jumio, Onfido, and Persona for this talent — and Plaid's advantage is the network effect of 12,000+ bank connections feeding fraud signals back into the identity product. Position yourself as someone who understands both the ML and the regulatory side.
