Visa's $35B+ revenue and consistent stock appreciation (NYSE: V) make the equity a powerful wealth-building mechanism, particularly for ML engineers who join during the Protocol's build phase and develop irreplaceable expertise in AI-agent trust modeling. The company competes directly with Google DeepMind, OpenAI, Meta AI, and hedge fund quant teams for this talent.
