### Trusted Agent Protocol — The Standard-Bearer Premium

**Lever 1 — Architectural Ownership:** "The Trusted Agent Protocol requires someone to own the end-to-end architecture of AI-initiated transaction flows — from agent authentication through authorization and settlement. That's the role I'd be stepping into. I'd like the RSU grant to reflect architectural ownership, not just feature delivery. I'm looking for the upper half of the equity band — $240,000 or above over four years."

**Lever 2 — Standard-Bearer Positioning:** "Visa has framed Trusted Agent Protocol engineers as Standard-Bearers — the people who define how AI and secure payments coexist. I take that framing seriously, and I'd like my compensation to match it. A base of $220,000 in Foster City positions me at the level where I'm not fielding recruiter calls from Stripe or Google every quarter."

**Lever 3 — Cross-Network Impact:** "Every design decision I make on the Trusted Agent Protocol will propagate across Visa's network — 4B+ cards, 200+ countries, 65,000 TPS. **Lever 4 — Retention Cliff Risk Mitigation:** "I want to be transparent: the one-year vesting cliff creates a retention risk for both of us.
