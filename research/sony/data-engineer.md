**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at Sony build pipelines processing data from PlayStation Network (100M+ MAU gamer behavioral data), Sony's entertainment platforms (Crunchyroll anime streaming, Sony LIV), and Sony Semiconductor Solutions (image sensor manufacturing data). The gaming analytics pipeline -- tracking player engagement, purchase behavior, and social interactions -- feeds into recommendation systems, player lifecycle models, and game publisher analytics.

PlayStation generates one of the world's richest gaming behavioral datasets: play sessions, trophy achievements, purchase patterns, social graph data, and streaming viewing behavior. Data engineers who can build pipelines for this data develop gaming analytics expertise that is highly valued.
