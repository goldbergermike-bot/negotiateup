Sony AI, the company's dedicated research lab, publishes at top venues and focuses on gastronomy, gaming, and sensing AI.

ML/AI Engineers at SIE work on game AI systems, player behavior prediction, recommendation models for PlayStation Store, and cloud gaming optimization. Compensation is competitive with gaming industry AI roles and has improved as Sony competes with Microsoft, EA, and Epic for AI talent.
