**Negotiation DNA**

Senior Software Engineers at ASML are the backbone of the lithography software stack — owning critical subsystems that control wafer stage positioning, beam alignment, and computational lithography algorithms. With ASML's absolute monopoly on EUV technology and a €38B+ backlog guaranteeing years of production demand, senior engineers are not building disposable products; they are maintaining and advancing the only machines capable of producing sub-5nm chips. Every major semiconductor fab on Earth is an ASML customer, and every one of them is waiting in line. (Sources: ASML 2024 Annual Report; Glassdoor Senior SWE data; Levels.fyi ASML.)

**€38B+ Backlog Monopoly — The Absolute Stability Premium**

ASML can't be disrupted, can't be replaced, and has years of guaranteed revenue — this stability justifies top-of-market base salary. As a Senior Software Engineer, you are a high-leverage individual contributor whose expertise directly impacts machine throughput and yield — metrics that translate to billions in customer revenue. The €38B+ backlog means ASML is not speculating on market demand; it has committed orders from every leading chipmaker. In Veldhoven, where the ASML campus has transformed a small Dutch city into a global tech hub, housing prices and living costs have surged. The Eindhoven metro area now commands rents competitive with Amsterdam for quality housing near the ASML campus. Senior engineers should push base to the €100K–€105K range, citing both monopoly stability and Veldhoven's rising cost of living. In Wilton, CT, leverage Connecticut's high state taxes and Fairfield County costs to target $200K+ base. In San Jose, benchmark against local semiconductor peers like Applied Materials and Lam Research at equivalent seniority.
