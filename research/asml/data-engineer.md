**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at ASML build the data infrastructure that processes, stores, and distributes the most valuable manufacturing data in the world. Every EUV machine generates terabytes of high-frequency sensor data — wafer alignment telemetry, optical performance metrics, thermal profiles, and predictive maintenance signals. Because ASML has a monopoly on EUV lithography, this data represents the complete global dataset for advanced chipmaking — and it flows exclusively through ASML's data pipelines. The €38B+ backlog guarantees this data volume will only grow as new machines are deployed. (Sources: ASML 2024 Annual Report; Glassdoor ASML Data Engineer data; Levels.fyi data engineering entries.)

**Level Mapping:** ASML Data Engineer maps to Google L4 Data Engineer, Meta Data Engineer IC4, Apple Data Engineer, Microsoft Data Engineer 61–63, and semiconductor industry Senior Data Infrastructure Engineer.

**€38B+ Backlog Monopoly — The Absolute Stability Premium**

ASML can't be disrupted, can't be replaced, and has years of guaranteed revenue — this stability justifies top-of-market base salary. As a Data Engineer, you are building the infrastructure for a data monopoly that mirrors ASML's machine monopoly. The €38B+ backlog means the volume of machine telemetry data flowing through your pipelines is guaranteed to grow year over year — every new EUV machine deployed adds to the data footprint. Your data infrastructure is the foundation for ASML's growing software and analytics business, which is becoming an increasingly important revenue stream alongside machine sales. In Veldhoven, data engineers compete with demand from Eindhoven's broader tech ecosystem — Philips, NXP, and a growing startup community. Housing near the ASML campus has become particularly competitive, with rental costs climbing steadily. Push for €80K–€82K base. In the US, data engineers with high-frequency industrial data pipeline experience (as opposed to typical web-scale batch processing) are scarce — target $168K–$170K base.
