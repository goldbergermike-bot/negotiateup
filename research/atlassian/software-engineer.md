---

Atlassian (NASDAQ: TEAM, $4.4B+ ARR) builds the collaboration backbone for over 300,000 enterprise customers and 10M+ monthly active users through Jira, Confluence, Trello, Bitbucket, Jira Service Management, and Loom. Their "Team Anywhere" distributed work policy eliminates HQ requirements, and their compensation model mirrors the Salesforce/Snowflake approach: no annual bonus, with all variable compensation loaded into generous initial RSU grants (Atlassian RSUs -- NASDAQ: TEAM). As a Software Engineer, you build the platform foundations that power Atlassian's cloud-first future -- including the Feb 2, 2026 rollout of Guard Premium and Rovo AI, which redefines every engineer's role as an AI Orchestra conductor who manages both the security governance layer and the autonomous AI agent layer simultaneously.

#### Global Levers

1. **No Bonus = All Negotiation Goes to Atlassian RSUs (NASDAQ: TEAM).** Atlassian's zero-bonus model means your entire variable compensation is equity. Push for 20-30% above the initial RSU offer: "Since there's no bonus component, I need the Atlassian RSU grant to carry my full variable comp. My competing offer at [Datadog/Salesforce] includes a 15% bonus plus equity -- I need TEAM RSUs to bridge that gap."

2. **Guard Premium + Rovo AI Orchestra Positioning (Feb 2, 2026 Rollout).** The Feb 2, 2026 launch of Guard Premium and Rovo AI makes every SWE a dual-role AI Orchestra conductor. Frame this: "I understand the Conductor/Instrument duality that the Guard Premium and Rovo rollout creates. I can orchestrate Rovo AI agents for workflow automation while building the security governance layer that Guard Premium requires -- this AI Orchestra capability is exactly what Atlassian needs post-rollout."

3. **Cloud Migration Revenue Engine.** Over 80% of Atlassian's new revenue growth comes from cloud products. Cloud-native architecture experience is premium: "My experience building cloud-native microservices directly maps to Atlassian's highest-revenue initiative. Every engineer who accelerates cloud migration compounds ARR growth."

4. **Team Anywhere = No Geographic Discount.** Atlassian pays the same regardless of where you live within a country. Use this to neutralize any location-based pushback: "Atlassian's Team Anywhere policy means my comp should reflect role value, not zip code. An engineer in Austin earns the same as one in San Francisco -- this is a feature of Atlassian's model, not a negotiation point."
