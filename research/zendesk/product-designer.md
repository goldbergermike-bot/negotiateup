#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Zendesk

Zendesk's design heritage — the company pioneered the modern, friendly helpdesk aesthetic — means design remains a core competitive differentiator. Product Designers must now evolve this design language for the AI-first era: designing how AI recommendations appear to agents, how automated resolution feels to customers, and how human-AI collaboration works in practice.
