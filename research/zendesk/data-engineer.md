Data Engineers at Zendesk build pipelines that process one of the largest CX datasets in the world. This data — billions of customer interactions across every channel and industry — is the foundation for AI model training, customer analytics, and business intelligence. Under PE ownership, this data infrastructure must be both powerful and cost-efficient.
