**$20B NVIDIA Deal — The Control-through-Licensing Premium**

The $20B NVIDIA deal fundamentally changes the negotiation calculus for Senior Software Engineers at Groq. NVIDIA's decision to license Groq's LPU technology — rather than replicate it — validates that the inference speed advantage is architecturally defensible. For senior engineers, this means your work on performance-critical systems, API design, and cross-platform compatibility is directly tied to the revenue engine that drives Groq's valuation. The "control-through-licensing" model means Groq maintains engineering ownership of the core technology while NVIDIA handles distribution, creating a flywheel where every engineering improvement compounds across NVIDIA's massive customer base.
