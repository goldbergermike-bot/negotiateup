#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Asana

Engineering Managers at Asana lead teams in one of the most intentionally designed engineering cultures in Silicon Valley. Co-founder Dustin Moskovitz has built Asana with explicit focus on team effectiveness, work-life balance, and engineering excellence. EMs must navigate this culture while delivering competitive products in a market with strong rivals.

Asana's engineering culture values clarity, distributed decision-making, and long-term thinking. EMs who can build teams that embody these values while shipping at competitive velocity are highly valued.
