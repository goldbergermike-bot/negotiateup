#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Asana

PMs at Asana must translate the Work Graph's technical capabilities into product features that compete against Monday.com, Smartsheet, and Microsoft Planner. The strategic challenge is differentiation — proving that Asana's graph-based approach to work management delivers superior outcomes. PMs who can articulate and deliver this differentiation are the growth engine.
