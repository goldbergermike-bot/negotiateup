#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Asana

DevOps at Asana supports a platform that teams depend on for daily work coordination. Downtime disrupts work management for millions of users across every time zone. The reliability bar is high, and DevOps engineers who can maintain this while enabling rapid feature delivery are essential to Asana's competitive position.
