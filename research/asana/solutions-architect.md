Solutions Architects at Asana help enterprise customers design Work Graph implementations that map to their organizational structure — teams, projects, goals, and portfolios. The ability to translate complex enterprise workflows into Asana's graph-based model requires both technical architecture skills and deep understanding of how organizations coordinate work.
