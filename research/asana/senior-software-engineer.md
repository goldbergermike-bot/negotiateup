#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Asana

Senior Software Engineers at Asana are the technical backbone of the Work Graph platform. At this level, you own subsystem design decisions, drive architectural reviews, and mentor junior engineers while shipping features that millions of teams depend on daily. Asana's engineering culture emphasizes craftsmanship and long-term thinking, making Senior SWEs the primary drivers of technical quality.

Asana's mission-driven culture — founded by Dustin Moskovitz with the explicit goal of helping humanity thrive — creates a strong retention dynamic. Senior engineers who align with this mission and combine it with technical excellence are highly valued.
