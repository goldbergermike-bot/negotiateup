#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Asana

Data Scientists at Asana work with a fundamentally unique dataset: the Work Graph captures how millions of teams coordinate work — task completion patterns, project dependency structures, resource allocation decisions, and collaboration dynamics. This graph-structured data enables analytical approaches that are impossible with flat datasets, making Asana data science work both more interesting and more impactful than at typical SaaS companies.
