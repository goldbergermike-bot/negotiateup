#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Asana

TPMs at Asana manage programs within a culture that values distributed decision-making, clarity, and long-term thinking. This means TPMs must be skilled at influence without authority, cross-team alignment, and program visibility — all without relying on top-down mandates. The cultural fit premium at Asana is real and valuable.
