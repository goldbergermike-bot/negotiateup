**HPE Unified Sales Comp — The Synergy Accelerator Premium**

Technical Program Managers are among the most critical "Synergy Accelerators" in the HPE-Juniper integration because every integration workstream — engineering, product, sales, operations — needs a TPM to drive it forward. The unified sales compensation structure launched in January 2026 created dozens of new cross-functional programs that require coordination: unified product launches, sales enablement training, customer migration planning, and portfolio rationalization. Each of these programs needs a TPM. The Mist AI 90% ticket reduction proves that Juniper's cross-functional execution model works, and the TPMs who drive that model deserve compensation that reflects their orchestration impact. Negotiate by quantifying the number of teams, timelines, and revenue streams you'll be coordinating. HPE historically offered TPMs stronger bonus targets (12–18%) and more generous equity, while Juniper's base salaries for TPMs were competitive. Push for the higher base from Juniper's legacy band and the stronger bonus and equity from HPE's structure. Additionally, argue that integration-focused TPM roles are inherently time-limited and high-stress — justify a sign-on bonus or retention grant as acknowledgment of this.
