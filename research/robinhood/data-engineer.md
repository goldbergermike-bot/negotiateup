#### Negotiation DNA

Robinhood Data Engineers (IC2-IC3) build the data infrastructure and pipelines that are the circulatory system of a platform serving 24M+ funded accounts. You architect the real-time and batch processing systems that ingest market data, user portfolio information, trading activity, transaction records, and financial signals — transforming raw data into the structured, reliable, and compliant datasets that power product features, ML models, business analytics, regulatory reporting, and executive decision-making. Without your data pipelines, nothing at Robinhood works — not the trading experience, not the AI features, not the compliance reports, and not the business metrics that drive the stock price.

The Rothera JV transforms the Data Engineer role from a single-product data builder into an ecosystem data architect. As Robinhood becomes an "Information and Capital Markets Ecosystem," data engineers must build unified data platforms that integrate data streams from equities, crypto, retirement, credit, cash management, and institutional services into a coherent data layer. This is not just more data — it is fundamentally more complex data, with different schemas, latency requirements, compliance obligations, and quality standards across each vertical. Data Engineers who can demonstrate multi-source financial data integration experience can negotiate Ecosystem-Critical premiums of $18K-$32K above standard offers.

The "Information" in "Information and Capital Markets Ecosystem" is data. Data Engineers are building the "Information" half of the Rothera vision — the data products, analytics platforms, and institutional data feeds that make Robinhood an information company, not just a trading app.

#### Rothera — The Ecosystem-Critical Premium

The mid-2026 Rothera Joint Venture transforms Robinhood from a commission-free trading application into an "Information and Capital Markets Ecosystem." For Data Engineers, this is especially significant because "Information" is literally half the ecosystem name — your data infrastructure IS the product:

- **Ecosystem Data Platform Architects Command $18K-$32K Premiums:** Data Engineers building the unified data platform that integrates streams from six product verticals — real-time market data, crypto chain data, retirement account data, credit card transaction data, cash management flows, and institutional data feeds — are classified as ecosystem-critical. These roles carry an $18K-$32K annual premium because the ecosystem's "Information" half cannot exist without their data infrastructure.

- **Multi-Vertical Data Integration Is 5x Pre-Rothera Complexity:** Pre-Rothera, a Data Engineer managed pipelines for one product. Post-Rothera, you manage pipelines across equities, crypto, retirement, credit, cash management, and institutional data — each with different schemas, latency requirements (sub-millisecond for trading, hourly for retirement), and regulatory data retention obligations. This 5x complexity justifies $265K-$292K TC at the IC3 level.

- **Institutional Data Products Are a New Revenue Line — Worth $12K-$22K in Premium:** The Rothera JV includes institutional data feeds and research products — new revenue streams built directly on the data platform. Data Engineers whose infrastructure enables these data products to exist have direct P&L impact on a new business line. Use this to negotiate an additional RSU uplift of $12K-$22K and a sign-on bonus of $20K-$38K.

- **Data Quality in Financial Services Is Regulatory-Grade — Scarcity Premium of $10K-$18K:** Financial data must meet regulatory standards for accuracy, completeness, timeliness, and auditability. Data Engineers who can build pipelines meeting SEC, FINRA, and banking data integrity requirements — not just "good enough" consumer analytics — are scarce. This regulatory data expertise adds $10K-$18K to your negotiable range.
