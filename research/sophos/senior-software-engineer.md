Senior Software Engineers at Sophos are technical leaders who own subsystem architecture across endpoint, network, and cloud security products. Sophos's Abingdon, UK headquarters and distributed global engineering teams (including offices in Vancouver, India, and US remote) create a role with significant autonomy and technical ownership.

Under Thoma Bravo's $3.9B ownership, Senior SWEs drive both product innovation and platform efficiency. The breadth of Sophos's product portfolio — endpoint, firewall, MDR, cloud security — creates diverse technical challenges at the senior level.
