#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Sophos

Product Designers at Sophos solve the challenge of making enterprise and SMB security management intuitive through Sophos Central. The platform manages endpoint security, firewalls, Wi-Fi, email, and mobile security from a single console. Designers must create experiences that work for both small business IT generalists and enterprise security teams.
