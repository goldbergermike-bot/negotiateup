**Ad-Free Monetization — The $600M+ Subscription Moat**

Discord generates $600M+ in annual revenue entirely from subscriptions (Nitro, Server Boosts, premium features) — zero advertising. As a Product Manager, this ad-free model is the most important lever in your negotiation: (1) Subscription revenue is more predictable and recession-resistant than ad revenue — your product decisions drive a revenue stream that doesn't evaporate when advertisers cut budgets, making your impact on the business more durable and measurable than PM impact at ad-dependent platforms. (2) Discord's user trust and engagement stay high because there are no ads degrading the experience — as a PM, you design product experiences that users love enough to pay for, which is a fundamentally different (and harder) challenge than optimizing ad load. This expertise is rare and valuable. (3) When comparing Discord equity to Meta or TikTok offers, candidates should argue that Discord's clean monetization model creates more sustainable long-term value — the $600M+ proves product-market fit without ad dependency, and PMs who drive subscription growth at pre-IPO companies have outsized equity upside.
