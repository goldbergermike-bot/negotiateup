**Ad-Free Monetization — The $600M+ Subscription Moat**

Discord generates $600M+ in annual revenue entirely from subscriptions (Nitro, Server Boosts, premium features) — zero advertising. As a Product Designer, this ad-free model fundamentally changes the nature of your work and your negotiation leverage: (1) Subscription revenue is more predictable and recession-resistant than ad revenue — your designs drive Nitro upgrade flows, premium feature experiences, and Server Boost interfaces that generate recurring revenue, not one-time ad impressions. This means your design impact compounds over time. (2) Discord's user trust and engagement stay high because there are no ads degrading the experience — you never have to compromise your design vision to accommodate ad placements, banner insertions, or sponsored content. Your canvas is clean, and your designs serve users, not advertisers. (3) When comparing Discord equity to Meta or TikTok offers, candidates should argue that Discord's clean monetization model creates more sustainable long-term value — the $600M+ proves product-market fit without ad dependency, and designers who craft premium subscription experiences directly contribute to the metrics that drive IPO valuation. At Discord, design isn't decoration — it's the primary conversion mechanism for a $600M+ subscription business.
