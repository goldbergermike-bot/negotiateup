**Negotiation DNA**

Senior Software Engineers at Navan are the backbone of the Concierge 2.0 platform — the senior ICs who own critical subsystems, mentor junior engineers, and drive the architectural decisions that determine whether Navan can scale reliably across the $1.5T corporate travel market. The Feb 2026 AI Rebooking launch elevated the complexity of Navan's systems enormously: real-time flight disruption detection, automatic itinerary reconstruction, payment reprocessing, and traveler notification all must execute flawlessly under pressure. Senior engineers who can own these end-to-end flows are extraordinarily valuable.

Navan's Reliability Architect positioning means that senior engineers aren't just writing features — they're building the infrastructure that makes 10,000+ companies trust Navan with their entire travel and expense operations. This is a differentiated skill set that commands a premium over generic senior engineering roles. The Ava AI assistant's evolution into a proactive travel concierge requires senior engineers with distributed systems expertise, ML pipeline experience, and a deep understanding of fault-tolerant architectures.

The pre-IPO equity story is particularly compelling at this level. With Navan valued at $9.4B and backed by a16z, Lightspeed, and Zeev Ventures, senior engineers who join now receive options that could appreciate significantly through an IPO. Candidates coming from Big Tech (Google, Meta, Stripe) with liquid RSU packages should negotiate aggressively on option grants and request valuation transparency, including the most recent 409A valuation and preferred share price, to properly evaluate the equity component.
