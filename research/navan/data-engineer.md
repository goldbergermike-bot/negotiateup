**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at Navan build the foundational data infrastructure that powers everything from Ava AI's intelligence to enterprise reporting to financial reconciliation. The platform generates massive volumes of structured and unstructured data: travel booking events, expense transactions, corporate card swipes, flight status updates, pricing signals, and traveler behavior patterns across 10,000+ companies. The Feb 2026 AI Rebooking launch intensified data engineering demands — real-time flight disruption data must be ingested, processed, and fed into ML models within seconds, while historical rebooking outcomes must be stored and queryable for model training and enterprise reporting.

The Reliability Architect positioning depends on data reliability: enterprise customers need accurate reporting on travel spend, policy compliance, and cost savings. Data Engineers who build the pipelines that produce these reports are directly responsible for the metrics that drive enterprise retention and expansion in the $1.5T corporate travel market. A reporting error or data pipeline failure doesn't just break a dashboard — it breaks the trust that Navan's Reliability Architect brand promises.

At a $9.4B private valuation, Data Engineers who join pre-IPO are building the data infrastructure that will support IPO-grade financial reporting, investor analytics, and public company governance requirements. The ability to build data pipelines that are both performant and auditable is a premium skill at this stage. Candidates should negotiate for data platform ownership scope and equity that reflects the foundational nature of their contribution to Navan's Concierge 2.0 platform.
