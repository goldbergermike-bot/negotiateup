**Negotiation DNA**

Technical Program Managers at Navan orchestrate the cross-functional execution that transforms ambitious product visions into shipped features. The Feb 2026 AI Rebooking launch was a masterclass in program management — coordinating ML engineers building disruption prediction models, backend engineers building the rebooking orchestration layer, payment engineers handling reprocessing flows, mobile engineers updating traveler interfaces, and operations teams training support staff — all under a fixed deadline with enterprise customer commitments. TPMs who can drive this kind of multi-team coordination across Navan's platform are invaluable.

The Reliability Architect positioning requires program-level thinking: Navan's promise to be the most reliable corporate travel platform in the $1.5T market depends on coordinated execution across engineering, infrastructure, QA, and customer success teams. TPMs define the release processes, quality gates, and cross-team dependencies that determine whether Concierge 2.0 features ship reliably. They also manage the enterprise launch programs that roll out new capabilities to 10,000+ companies, ensuring that each deployment meets the reliability standards that differentiate Navan from SAP Concur and Brex.

At a $9.4B private valuation, TPMs who join pre-IPO are building the program management infrastructure that will scale through IPO and beyond. The ability to manage cross-functional programs across engineering, product, design, and go-to-market teams is particularly valuable as Navan prepares for the operational rigor that public markets demand. Candidates should negotiate for scope that encompasses strategic programs (not just feature delivery) and equity that reflects the organizational multiplier effect of strong program management.
