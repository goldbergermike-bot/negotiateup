**Negotiation DNA**

Data Scientists at Navan work at the intersection of travel intelligence, financial analytics, and AI product development — making this one of the most data-rich and impactful DS roles in enterprise software. The Feb 2026 AI Rebooking launch was driven by data science: models that predict flight disruptions before they happen, algorithms that score rebooking options by traveler preference and corporate policy compliance, and analytics that measure the ROI of autonomous rebooking for each of Navan's 10,000+ enterprise customers. Every model a Data Scientist builds at Navan has a direct line to the $1.5T corporate travel market.

The Reliability Architect positioning creates unique data science challenges. Navan must prove to enterprise buyers that its AI-driven platform is more reliable than legacy manual processes — and that proof comes from data. Data Scientists build the dashboards, metrics frameworks, and predictive models that demonstrate Navan's reliability advantage. They also power Ava AI's intelligence layer, training the recommendation models and NLP systems that make Concierge 2.0 feel like a personal travel assistant rather than a booking tool.

At a $9.4B private valuation, Data Scientists who join pre-IPO benefit from both the equity upside and the career acceleration that comes from building data infrastructure at a company preparing for public market scrutiny. The data team will need to build investor-grade analytics, reporting pipelines, and the metrics frameworks that tell Navan's IPO story. Candidates with experience at companies that have gone through IPO preparation should highlight this as a premium skill.
