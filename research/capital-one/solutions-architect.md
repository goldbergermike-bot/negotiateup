**Negotiation DNA**

Solutions Architects at Capital One design the technical architecture for a fully cloud-native banking platform on AWS. Unlike peer banks where architects must navigate legacy mainframe systems, Capital One SAs work exclusively with modern cloud architectures, microservices, and managed services. The architecture challenge is designing systems that handle financial-grade reliability and regulatory compliance on public cloud infrastructure -- a problem that Capital One has solved more successfully than any other major bank.

Compensation follows the tech model: base plus RSUs (4-year vest) plus bonus (10-15%). Architecture compensation is at or above senior engineering levels and competitive with AWS, Google Cloud, and other tech company SA roles.

Competition comes from cloud providers (AWS, Google Cloud), tech companies, and other banks. Capital One's advantage is the architecture challenge: designing cloud-native banking systems at scale is a unique and career-defining experience.
