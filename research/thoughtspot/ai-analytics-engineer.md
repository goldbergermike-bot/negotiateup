**Negotiation DNA**

This is ThoughtSpot's signature engineering role. AI Analytics Engineers build the core AI-powered search and natural language query engine that differentiates ThoughtSpot from every other analytics platform. ThoughtSpot pioneered the "search-driven analytics" paradigm — letting business users ask data questions in natural language. With the integration of generative AI (ThoughtSpot Sage, powered by large language models), this role now sits at the intersection of NLP, data analytics, and enterprise AI. ThoughtSpot went public via direct listing in 2021 but was taken private again, meaning equity is private stock with liquidity dependent on a future event (IPO or acquisition). This creates asymmetric upside but also liquidity risk. [Source: ThoughtSpot AI Analytics Eng Comp 2025-2026]

**What Makes This Role Unique**

The AI Analytics Engineer works across ThoughtSpot's core AI stack:
- **Natural Language Query Engine:** Converting business questions into optimized SQL across heterogeneous data sources
- **ThoughtSpot Sage (GenAI):** Integrating LLM-powered analytics with enterprise data governance
- **Search Indexing:** Building the search infrastructure that enables sub-second query responses on massive datasets
- **Autonomous Analytics:** Developing AI-driven insights that proactively surface anomalies and trends
