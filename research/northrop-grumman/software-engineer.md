**Negotiation DNA**

SWEs work across space systems (satellite software, ground systems), autonomous systems (Global Hawk, Triton drones), missile defense (ICBM modernization), and cyber/intelligence systems. Software at Northrop spans embedded real-time systems for spacecraft, mission planning tools, ground station operations, and data processing for intelligence systems. The culture is traditional defense with strong work-life balance: 40-45 hour weeks, 10% annual bonus, RSUs, excellent 401k matching (up to 6%), pension eligibility, and stable long-term employment. Total comp is below FAANG ($130K-$210K vs. $200K-$350K) but the work-life balance, job stability, retirement benefits, and defense mission are the value proposition.
