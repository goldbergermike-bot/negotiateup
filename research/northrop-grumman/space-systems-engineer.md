**What Makes This Role Unique**

The Space Systems Engineer builds spacecraft and space-based defense systems:
- **National Security Space:** You build intelligence satellites that provide critical national security capabilities. Many programs are classified at TS/SCI level
- **Science Mission Spacecraft:** You build spacecraft for NASA deep space and Earth observation missions, continuing Northrop's legacy with James Webb
- **Spacecraft Full Lifecycle:** You work from concept through launch -- requirements, design, fabrication, integration, testing, and on-orbit operations
- **Space-Qualified Engineering:** Your designs must survive launch vibration, thermal vacuum, radiation, and operate for 10-15+ years without repair
- **Mission Assurance:** Every component you design is single-point-of-failure -- there is no recall once a satellite is in orbit
