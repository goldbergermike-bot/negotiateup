**Negotiation DNA**

Staff SWEs at Northrop (often called "Principal Engineers" or "Technical Leads") are the most senior software individual contributors. You set technical direction across programs within a business division, influence technology investment decisions, and represent Northrop's software capability during proposal technical evaluations. At Staff level, you've typically been in defense aerospace for 12+ years with deep domain expertise in space systems, autonomous vehicles, or intelligence systems. Staff engineers at Northrop are increasingly recruited by defense tech companies (Anduril, Shield AI, SpaceX) at 1.5-2x the compensation. Northrop's response has been to increase RSU grants and expand the Technical Fellow program to retain top talent. [Source: Northrop Staff SWE Comp 2025-2026]
