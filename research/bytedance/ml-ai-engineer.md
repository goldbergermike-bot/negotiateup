ML/AI Engineers at ByteDance are not a supporting function -- they are the business. This fundamental alignment between ML and revenue means ByteDance pays at the absolute top of the market for AI talent.

Compensation for ML engineers at ByteDance exceeds FAANG in many cases. The company has standing authorization to beat Google Brain, Meta FAIR, and OpenAI offers for exceptional ML talent, particularly for engineers with recommendation systems or large-scale ML training experience.
