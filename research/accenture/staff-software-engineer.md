**Negotiation DNA**

Staff Software Engineers at Accenture map to the Senior Manager / Managing Director level — the tier where Accenture's comp structure includes substantial RSU grants, higher bonus percentages (12-18%), and proximity to the Managing Director promotion (Accenture's equivalent of Partner). At this level, you're setting technical direction for practice areas, leading multiple delivery teams, and representing Accenture's technical capabilities in client pitches and analyst briefings. Accenture's RSU grants at this level are the largest comp lever — push hard on initial grant size. The Managing Director promotion (2-4 years away) brings comp to $500K-$1M+. **Global Levers**

1. **RSU Maximization:** "At this level, RSUs are the primary variable comp lever. I want the initial grant at $150K+ over 4 years. Accenture's stock has appreciated 15%+ annually — maximizing RSUs compounds wealth creation."

3. **MD Track Investment:** "I'm 2-4 years from Managing Director, where comp jumps to $500K-$1M+. My current comp should reflect this proximity and the firm's investment in my progression."
