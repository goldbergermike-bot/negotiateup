**Negotiation DNA**
Data Scientists at Zoom work with communication datasets of extraordinary scale and richness — billions of meeting minutes, chat interactions, phone calls, and collaboration events. The analytical opportunities span meeting engagement modeling, communication pattern analysis, platform quality optimization, customer lifecycle prediction, and the data science behind AI Companion features (meeting summarization quality, transcription accuracy, smart recommendation relevance). Few companies generate as much structured and unstructured communication data as Zoom.

Zoom compensates Data Scientists at competitive Bay Area rates, typically 10-15% above mid-cap SaaS peers. The company values Data Scientists who can bridge internal analytics (product usage, revenue optimization, experiment design) with product-embedded intelligence (features that surface insights to Zoom users directly). Data Scientists who contribute to product-facing features command higher compensation because their work directly influences user retention and premium tier adoption.

The role benefits from Zoom's position as a primary communication platform for businesses worldwide. The data is rich with behavioral signals — meeting frequency, engagement patterns, communication network structure — that enable sophisticated analytical modeling. For Data Scientists interested in human communication patterns at scale, Zoom offers datasets that are genuinely unique.
