#### Double-Trigger Acceleration Check

HubSpot M&A Protection — Double-Trigger Acceleration Check: HubSpot has been the subject of acquisition interest (Google was reportedly in talks). Before accepting, verify your RSU agreement includes double-trigger acceleration: if HubSpot is acquired AND you are terminated or role-changed within 12-24 months post-acquisition, 50-100% of your unvested RSUs accelerate immediately. Use this script: "Given the M&A environment for companies like HubSpot, I'd like to confirm my RSU agreement includes double-trigger acceleration provisions. Specifically, if HubSpot is acquired and my role is materially changed or eliminated within [12/18/24] months, I want [50/75/100]% of unvested RSUs to vest immediately." If HubSpot's standard agreement doesn't include this, negotiate it as a side letter — especially for senior roles.

#### Global Negotiation Levers

1. **Base Salary Lever — Platform Architecture Premium:** Staff Engineers own cross-hub architecture and platform-level decisions. If you have led platform teams, designed multi-tenant SaaS architectures, or driven CRM infrastructure at scale, push for the top of the band: "At my current role I architected [platform/system] serving [scale]. HubSpot's multi-hub CRM platform requires this same depth of platform thinking. I'm targeting $[target] base to reflect Staff-level platform ownership."

2. **Equity Lever — RSU Grant Maximization with M&A Upside:** Staff-level RSU grants ($290K-$465K/4yr) represent the largest wealth-building opportunity. With M&A potential, these grants carry significant upside. Push for top-of-band RSU and layer in double-trigger protections: "Given both my Staff-level scope and HubSpot's position as an acquisition target, I'd like the RSU grant at $[target] with explicit double-trigger acceleration. This protects both parties' interests and reflects the strategic value of this role."

3. **Bonus Lever — Performance Multiplier Negotiation:** Staff-level bonus ranges are 12-18%. Negotiate for the higher end with a guaranteed floor: "I'd like to clarify the bonus structure — specifically, I'm looking for a guaranteed minimum of 15% with a performance multiplier up to 18%, rather than a variable 12-18% range with discretionary payout."

4. **Title & Scope Lever — Distinguished Engineer Path:** If your background warrants, negotiate for explicit Distinguished Engineer promotion criteria and timeline: "Based on my industry impact — [publications, patents, open-source contributions, conference talks] — I'd like to discuss the Distinguished Engineer track and what a 12-18 month path looks like, with corresponding comp adjustments at promotion."

---

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** Staff Engineer is a leverage-rich position at HubSpot. You are rare, expensive to recruit, and hard to replace. Lead negotiations with your architectural vision for HubSpot's platform — specifically how you would unify the six-hub architecture and accelerate Breeze AI. Present a written technical vision document before comp discussions. Use the M&A angle aggressively at this level: with $290K-$465K in RSU at stake, double-trigger acceleration could mean $200K+ in protected equity. Get the side letter.
