#### Double-Trigger Acceleration Check

HubSpot M&A Protection — Double-Trigger Acceleration Check: HubSpot has been the subject of acquisition interest (Google was reportedly in talks). Before accepting, verify your RSU agreement includes double-trigger acceleration: if HubSpot is acquired AND you are terminated or role-changed within 12-24 months post-acquisition, 50-100% of your unvested RSUs accelerate immediately. Use this script: "Given the M&A environment for companies like HubSpot, I'd like to confirm my RSU agreement includes double-trigger acceleration provisions. Specifically, if HubSpot is acquired and my role is materially changed or eliminated within [12/18/24] months, I want [50/75/100]% of unvested RSUs to vest immediately." If HubSpot's standard agreement doesn't include this, negotiate it as a side letter — especially for senior roles.

#### Global Negotiation Levers

1. **Base Salary Lever — CRM Platform Complexity Premium:** HubSpot's unified CRM platform (Marketing + Sales + Service + CMS + Operations Hub) demands cross-domain engineering fluency. If you have experience building on multi-hub platforms, SaaS CRM systems, or API-first architectures, argue for top-quartile base: "My experience building across integrated SaaS platforms aligns with HubSpot's multi-hub architecture. I'd like my base to reflect the cross-domain complexity at $[target] rather than the midpoint."

2. **Equity Lever — RSU Refresh Acceleration:** HubSpot offers competitive annual RSU refreshes. Negotiate a first-year refresh guarantee or an accelerated vesting schedule (e.g., 33/33/34 over 3 years instead of standard 4-year): "Given HubSpot's strong stock performance and my immediate contributions to [specific Hub], I'd like to discuss either an accelerated vesting schedule or a guaranteed Year 1 RSU refresh of $[amount]."

3. **Signing Bonus Lever — Unvested Equity Bridge:** If leaving unvested equity elsewhere, request a signing bonus to bridge the gap. HubSpot signing bonuses for SWE roles typically range $15K-$35K: "I'm forfeiting $[amount] in unvested equity at my current employer. A signing bonus of $[amount] would make the transition feasible and reflect HubSpot's commitment to competitive offers."

4. **Remote-First Lever — Location-Agnostic Band Negotiation:** HubSpot's remote-first model means you may be able to negotiate Cambridge-level compensation while living in a lower cost-of-living area. Push for HQ-benchmarked pay: "Since HubSpot is remote-first and the role's impact is location-independent, I'd like my compensation benchmarked to Cambridge MA bands rather than my local market."

---

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** Lead with HubSpot's "Grow Better" philosophy — frame every ask as investment in mutual growth. Open with competing offers from Salesforce, Atlassian, or comparable CRM/SaaS companies. Anchor on total compensation rather than base alone. Request the full offer letter and RSU agreement before your negotiation call so you can identify gaps in equity structure, vesting schedule, and M&A protections. Always negotiate in writing after verbal discussions to create a paper trail.
