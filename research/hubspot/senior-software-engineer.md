#### Double-Trigger Acceleration Check

HubSpot M&A Protection — Double-Trigger Acceleration Check: HubSpot has been the subject of acquisition interest (Google was reportedly in talks). Before accepting, verify your RSU agreement includes double-trigger acceleration: if HubSpot is acquired AND you are terminated or role-changed within 12-24 months post-acquisition, 50-100% of your unvested RSUs accelerate immediately. Use this script: "Given the M&A environment for companies like HubSpot, I'd like to confirm my RSU agreement includes double-trigger acceleration provisions. Specifically, if HubSpot is acquired and my role is materially changed or eliminated within [12/18/24] months, I want [50/75/100]% of unvested RSUs to vest immediately." If HubSpot's standard agreement doesn't include this, negotiate it as a side letter — especially for senior roles.

#### Global Negotiation Levers

1. **Base Salary Lever — Multi-Hub Architecture Premium:** At the senior level, HubSpot expects engineers to work across hub boundaries. If you bring cross-platform SaaS experience, API gateway expertise, or have shipped features touching multiple product surfaces, demand top-quartile base: "My track record of delivering cross-platform features in multi-product SaaS environments maps directly to HubSpot's multi-hub architecture. I'm targeting $[target] base to reflect the senior scope and cross-hub impact."

2. **Equity Lever — Competing Offer RSU Arbitrage:** Senior SWE is the level where HubSpot RSU grants become meaningful ($165K-$278K/4yr). Use competing offers from Salesforce, Datadog, or FAANG companies to push toward the top of the RSU band: "My competing offer includes $[amount] in RSU over 4 years. To make HubSpot's total comp competitive, I'd like the RSU component increased to $[target], reflecting both market rates and my expected contribution to Breeze AI and [specific Hub]."

3. **Signing Bonus Lever — Vesting Cliff Bridge:** If you are within 3-6 months of a significant vest at your current employer, negotiate a signing bonus that covers the forfeited amount. HubSpot signing bonuses at Senior SWE range $25K-$50K: "I have $[amount] vesting in [month]. A signing bonus of $[amount] bridges this gap and allows me to join HubSpot without a compensation penalty during the transition."

4. **Scope & Impact Lever — Tech Lead Path Negotiation:** If you are borderline Staff/Tech Lead, negotiate explicit promotion timelines or a Tech Lead title with corresponding comp: "Based on my scope of influence at [current employer] — leading [X-person] teams and owning [feature/system] — I believe the Tech Lead designation and corresponding band is appropriate. Can we discuss aligning the level and comp accordingly?"

---

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** At the Senior SWE level, your leverage is highest when you can demonstrate cross-hub value. Map your experience to at least two of HubSpot's six hubs (Marketing, Sales, Service, CMS, Operations, Commerce). Present a 90-day impact plan that shows immediate value. Use the M&A angle to push for equity protections — if Google or another acquirer moves on HubSpot, your double-trigger clause becomes the most valuable part of your package. Always get the full RSU agreement in writing before signing.
