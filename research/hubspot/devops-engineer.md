#### Double-Trigger Acceleration Check

HubSpot M&A Protection — Double-Trigger Acceleration Check: HubSpot has been the subject of acquisition interest (Google was reportedly in talks). Before accepting, verify your RSU agreement includes double-trigger acceleration: if HubSpot is acquired AND you are terminated or role-changed within 12-24 months post-acquisition, 50-100% of your unvested RSUs accelerate immediately. Use this script: "Given the M&A environment for companies like HubSpot, I'd like to confirm my RSU agreement includes double-trigger acceleration provisions. Specifically, if HubSpot is acquired and my role is materially changed or eliminated within [12/18/24] months, I want [50/75/100]% of unvested RSUs to vest immediately." If HubSpot's standard agreement doesn't include this, negotiate it as a side letter — especially for senior roles.

#### Global Negotiation Levers

1. **Base Salary Lever — Platform Scale Premium:** HubSpot's platform serves 200K+ customers across six product Hubs. DevOps engineers managing infrastructure at this scale should negotiate accordingly: "My experience operating [SaaS platform] at [scale — requests/sec, # customers, data volume] maps directly to HubSpot's multi-hub infrastructure challenges. I'm targeting $[target] base to reflect the platform-critical nature of this role."

2. **Equity Lever — Reliability = Revenue Protection:** Downtime directly impacts HubSpot's ARR. Frame RSU negotiation in terms of revenue protection: "DevOps engineers are the last line of defense for HubSpot's $2.6B+ ARR. My track record of [X nines uptime / Y% incident reduction / Z% deployment velocity improvement] justifies an RSU grant at $[target] over 4 years — this is a direct investment in revenue protection."

3. **Signing Bonus Lever — On-Call Transition Compensation:** DevOps engineers carry on-call responsibilities. If you are leaving a role where you received on-call pay, negotiate a signing bonus that accounts for this: "My current role includes $[amount] in annual on-call compensation. A signing bonus of $[amount] bridges this gap during my first year at HubSpot and accounts for forfeited equity of $[amount]."

4. **On-Call & Operational Lever — Incident Compensation Negotiation:** Negotiate explicit on-call compensation terms, incident response pay, and operational burden limits: "I'd like to understand and formalize the on-call rotation structure, including compensation for off-hours incidents, maximum on-call frequency, and escalation policies. Specifically, I'm looking for [specific on-call terms] as part of my offer."

---

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** DevOps Engineers should emphasize the revenue-protection angle: every minute of downtime costs HubSpot money. Quantify your reliability track record (uptime percentages, MTTR reductions, deployment frequency improvements). The Breeze AI infrastructure angle adds significant leverage — AI model serving requires specialized infrastructure (GPU orchestration, low-latency serving, ML pipeline automation) that most DevOps engineers lack. If you bring this AI infrastructure experience, push for 15-20% above the standard band. Use competing offers from Datadog, HashiCorp, or cloud providers (AWS, GCP, Azure).
