**Negotiation DNA**

PMs are expected to be deeply technical (writing SQL, understanding data pipelines), sit in customer war rooms, and drive adoption by embedding with customer teams. The AIP product is the highest-leverage PM role: defining how enterprises deploy AI agents on their own data using Palantir's platform. This is enterprise AI productization — the 137% US Commercial growth is driven by AIP adoption that PMs directly enable. No bonus; equity-heavy. Intensity is extreme — PMs often travel 40%+ of their time to customer sites. [Source: Palantir PM Comp 2025-2026]
