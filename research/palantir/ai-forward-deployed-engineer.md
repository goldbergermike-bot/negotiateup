**Negotiation DNA**

This is Palantir's signature role — the AI Forward Deployed Engineer (AI FDE). The AI FDE is a role that doesn't exist at any other company: you embed directly in customer environments (DOD, intelligence community, Fortune 100 enterprises) and deploy AIP AI agents on their data, in their security perimeter, solving their most critical problems. You are simultaneously an ML engineer, a solutions architect, a customer success engineer, and a product strategist — all in one role, all in the customer's environment, all at Palantir's 60+ hour intensity. The AI FDE is the delivery mechanism for AIP's 137% US Commercial growth: every new AI agent deployment that drives AIP adoption starts with an AI FDE embedding with the customer. This is the most intense, most customer-facing, most impactful individual contributor role in enterprise AI. [Source: Palantir AI FDE Team 2026]

**What Makes This Role Unique**

The AI Forward Deployed Engineer operates at the intersection of ML engineering, customer deployment, and business strategy:
- **Customer-Embedded AI Deployment:** You live on-site with customers (50%+ travel), deploying AIP AI agents on their most sensitive data in their most critical workflows
- **Full-Stack AI Engineering:** You build end-to-end: data preparation, model selection, prompt engineering, guardrail configuration, agent orchestration, and production monitoring — all in the customer's environment
- **Business Value Translation:** You translate customer business problems into AI agent solutions — understanding the customer's domain (defense, healthcare, energy, financial services) deeply enough to design effective AI workflows
- **Classified Environment AI:** For government customers, you deploy AI agents in classified, air-gapped environments — building AI solutions without internet access or cloud APIs
- **Prototype-to-Production:** You build the initial AI prototype that demonstrates value, then architect the path to production-scale deployment
