### AI-Native Design Product Strategy Lever

PMs who can define the AI design product strategy are Figma's most critical product hires. This means understanding what designers actually need from AI (not just what's technically possible), designing AI features that enhance rather than diminish creative agency, and navigating the sensitive relationship between AI automation and professional design.
