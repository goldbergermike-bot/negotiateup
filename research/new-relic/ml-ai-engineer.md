## Negotiation DNA

Since the 2023 take-private by TPG and Francisco Partners, New Relic has made AI its primary growth vector, investing heavily in ML infrastructure, LLM integration, and intelligent automation. Compensation includes stock options (not RSUs) with a 4-year vesting schedule, with ML/AI Engineers receiving some of the largest option grants in the company due to the strategic importance of the role. The talent market for ML/AI engineers with production systems experience is extremely competitive — FAANG companies, well-funded AI startups, and observability competitors are all bidding aggressively for this profile. Your negotiation leverage is substantial because you are building the exact features that New Relic's entire go-to-market strategy depends on.
