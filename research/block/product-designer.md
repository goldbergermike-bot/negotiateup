**Negotiation DNA**

Product Designers at Block in 2026 face a design challenge unlike anything in traditional fintech: designing the interaction layer between humans and autonomous AI agents. Since the December 2025 launch of the Agentic AI Foundation, Block's design org has pivoted from screen-centric product design to agent-centric experience design — defining how Square merchants interact with autonomous onboarding agents, how Cash App users trust and oversee autonomous financial assistants, and how agent actions are surfaced transparently in interfaces that handle real money.

The February 2026 workforce reduction of 10% compressed the design org into a Lean Elite team where each designer owns broader product surfaces. Block eliminated specialist design roles (motion, illustration, brand) and consolidated around full-stack product designers who can own end-to-end agent experiences. This means fewer designers, each with significantly more influence over how autonomous agents present to Block's tens of millions of Square merchants and Cash App users.

Block's $22B+ revenue depends on merchant and consumer trust — and trust design for autonomous financial agents is an emerging discipline with very few experienced practitioners. The Bitcoin treasury strategy and TBD's decentralized protocols add additional design complexity around crypto-native interfaces. Candidates who can demonstrate experience designing for AI-driven products, conversational interfaces, or trust-sensitive financial experiences have outsized negotiation leverage in the post-restructuring Lean Elite design org.
