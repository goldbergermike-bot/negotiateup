#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Render

Render is a private startup headquartered in San Francisco that builds a modern cloud platform designed as the successor to Heroku. The platform provides web services, static sites, background workers, cron jobs, managed PostgreSQL, Redis, and private networking — deployed directly from Git repositories.

Data Scientists at Render use platform telemetry and usage data to optimize resource allocation, inform pricing models, predict capacity needs, and understand developer behavior. At a usage-based pricing company, data science directly impacts revenue optimization and infrastructure efficiency.

Render competes with Heroku, Fly.io, Vercel, Railway, and major cloud providers. Data-driven insights about developer adoption, churn, and platform usage patterns are essential for competitive positioning and growth.
