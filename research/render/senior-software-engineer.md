### Senior Software Engineer — Render Salary Negotiation Guide

**Negotiation DNA**: Senior Software Engineers at Render own major platform subsystems — container orchestration, build systems, managed databases, or networking — with the ownership and impact that comes from building a modern cloud platform at a small, focused startup.

#### Lever 3: Equity Stake — "I expect meaningful options reflecting both my impact and the startup risk."
#### Lever 4: SF Competition — "I'm evaluating offers from SF startups and Big Tech. Render's equity must compensate for the risk."
