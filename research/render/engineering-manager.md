#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Render

Render is a private startup headquartered in San Francisco building the modern successor to Heroku. The platform provides web services, static sites, background workers, cron jobs, managed PostgreSQL, Redis, and private networking — all deployed directly from Git with zero-configuration.

Engineering Managers at Render lead small, high-impact teams in a startup environment. At Render's size, EMs are player-coaches — contributing technically while building team culture, hiring, and managing delivery. The EM role at a startup is more demanding than at large companies because there are fewer support structures, smaller teams with higher per-person impact, and the need for both technical depth and management breadth.

Render competes for engineering management talent with Big Tech (where base salaries and RSUs are higher) and other well-funded startups. The startup equity upside, technical impact, and platform ownership create the primary value proposition.
