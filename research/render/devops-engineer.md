#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Render

Render is a private startup headquartered in San Francisco building a modern cloud platform that eliminates DevOps complexity for developers. The platform offers git-push-to-deploy for web services, static sites, background workers, cron jobs, managed PostgreSQL, Redis, and private networking.

DevOps Engineers at Render handle the internal infrastructure automation that makes the "zero DevOps" customer experience possible. This includes maintaining the build infrastructure, deployment pipelines, monitoring systems, incident response tooling, and infrastructure-as-code that runs the platform. The irony is that Render's DevOps-free experience requires sophisticated DevOps engineering internally.

At a small startup, DevOps engineers own the entire operational stack — there are no separate SRE, platform, or infrastructure teams to lean on.
