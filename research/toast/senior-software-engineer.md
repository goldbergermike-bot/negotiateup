**Negotiation DNA**

Senior Software Engineers at Toast are the technical backbone of a platform that has grown from a point-of-sale terminal into the restaurant industry's definitive operating system. With over 120,000 restaurant locations depending on Toast's infrastructure for payments, operations, staffing, and now AI-driven intelligence through Toast IQ, senior engineers own critical systems where downtime directly impacts merchant revenue. The Feb 10 2026 Instacart Partnership has added a new dimension: senior engineers are now designing the integration architecture that connects Toast's restaurant OS with Instacart's delivery network, creating the industry's first unified dine-in-to-delivery technology stack.

At this level, Toast expects engineers to drive technical strategy, mentor junior developers, and make architectural decisions that scale across the entire 120,000+ location footprint. The company's $4B+ revenue and $20B+ market cap position it as a mature growth-stage company where senior engineers have outsized impact. The Hospitality Intelligence transformation — powered by Toast IQ — means senior engineers are building the AI and analytics infrastructure that will define Toast's next revenue chapter. This is not a maintenance role; it is a greenfield opportunity wrapped in the stability of a publicly traded company. Candidates with experience in real-time systems, payments infrastructure, machine learning pipelines, or large-scale SaaS platforms carry exceptional negotiation leverage.
