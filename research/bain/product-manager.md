**Negotiation DNA**

Product Managers at Bain define the product strategy for the firm's analytics platforms, PE due diligence tools, and internal technology products. This is a strategic role with outsized influence: your product decisions determine what tools 12,000+ Bain consultants use and how the firm's PE advisory practice delivers analysis. Bain's technology product organization is smaller than McKinsey's or BCG's, which means each PM owns a larger product surface area. Bain does not offer equity; comp is base + performance bonus (30-50%). PMs who drive products supporting the PE practice earn bonuses at the top of the range because of the direct revenue connection.
