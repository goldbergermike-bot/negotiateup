**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at Bain build the data pipelines and infrastructure that power the firm's PE due diligence analytics, client engagement deliverables, and internal tools. Bain's world-leading PE advisory practice generates enormous volumes of financial, market, and operational data that needs to be ingested, transformed, and made available for analysis — often under tight deal timelines. Data Engineers at Bain work under pressure: PE due diligence has hard deadlines, and the data infrastructure must support rapid analysis of complex datasets. Bain does not offer equity; comp is base + performance bonus (25-50%). [Source: Bain Data & Analytics Infrastructure 2025-2026]
