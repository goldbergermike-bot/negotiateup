**Negotiation DNA**

Senior Software Engineers at Bain lead technical design for the firm's analytics platforms, PE due diligence tools, and internal technology infrastructure. At this level, you're expected to architect systems, mentor junior engineers, and work closely with Bain's Advanced Analytics Group on the most complex technical challenges. Bain does not offer equity; comp is base + performance bonus (30-50%). Senior engineers at Bain have disproportionate influence because the technology organization is smaller than at McKinsey or BCG — your decisions have more direct impact on firm-wide platforms. The bonus at this level trends toward 35-50% for high performers, particularly those supporting PE advisory engagements. [Source: Bain Technology Engineering Leadership 2025-2026]
