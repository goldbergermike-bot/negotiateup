**Negotiation DNA**

Design at MongoDB is developer experience design at infrastructure scale — you're designing the Atlas UI, query builders, data visualization tools, and now the Voyage AI integration interfaces that 2M+ monthly active developers interact with. The design challenge is unique: making distributed database operations (sharding, replication, vector search configuration) intuitive for developers who aren't database experts. With the Voyage AI acquisition, there's a new design surface: embedding model configuration, vector search query building, and RAG pipeline visualization. MongoDB's no-bonus model means equity is your only lever beyond base. [Source: MongoDB Design Comp 2025-2026]

**Global Levers**

1. **Developer Experience at Scale:** "I'm designing for 2M+ monthly active developers on Atlas. My design decisions directly impact developer adoption, activation, and retention — that's measurable PLG impact."
2. **AI Data Layer UX:** "The Voyage AI integration creates entirely new design surfaces: embedding model configuration, vector search query builders, RAG pipeline visualization. I'm designing the UX for a new product category."
3. **Complexity-to-Simplicity Translation:** "Making distributed database operations intuitive is one of the hardest design challenges in infrastructure. Sharding configuration, replication topology, index optimization — translating this complexity into approachable UX is rare design skill."
4. **No-Bonus Equity Push:** Push for 20-25% more RSUs. The design team at MongoDB is small — each designer has outsized influence. Frame scarcity: "As one of a small design team covering Atlas's full surface area, my impact per designer is higher than at larger companies."

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** "For a design role shaping the developer experience for 2M+ Atlas developers — including the new Voyage AI integration surfaces — I'm targeting $175K in RSUs over 4 years. MongoDB will counter at $155K-$170K RSUs — accept at $160K+.
