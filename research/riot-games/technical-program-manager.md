**Negotiation DNA**

Technical Program Managers at Riot Games coordinate complex, cross-functional programs across game development, platform engineering, and live operations. Riot's TPM role is distinguished by the player-first culture — TPMs don't just manage timelines, they actively protect quality and player experience by ensuring teams have the time and resources to ship excellent products. Riot is famously resistant to crunch culture compared to other gaming companies, and TPMs play a key role in maintaining this standard while still delivering on time. TPMs on patch delivery, esports infrastructure, platform migrations, and new title launches handle programs that directly impact 150M+ players. The TPM function at Riot is relatively small, giving each TPM significant scope and visibility. [Source: Riot Games Program Management 2025]
