**Negotiation DNA**
Product Designers at SAP are part of one of the largest enterprise UX organizations in the world. SAP has invested heavily in design since the launch of SAP Fiori and the Horizon design system, recognizing that user experience is a critical differentiator in enterprise software adoption. Designers here work on products used daily by millions of business users — from warehouse managers and procurement specialists to CFOs and HR directors. The challenge is making extraordinarily complex business processes feel intuitive, which is a design problem unlike anything in consumer tech.

SAP compensates Product Designers competitively within the enterprise software tier, though below consumer tech companies like Apple, Google, or Airbnb where design carries premium brand cachet. The negotiation opportunity lies in SAP's increasing recognition that design quality directly drives cloud adoption metrics — customers who find SAP products easy to use are more likely to migrate to cloud and less likely to churn. This gives designers direct leverage tied to business outcomes.

SAP's design culture has matured significantly, with dedicated design thinking studios, a strong design research practice, and executive support from the C-suite. The company's AppHaus network provides collaborative design spaces worldwide. For designers who want to solve genuinely complex problems at global scale with strong organizational support, SAP offers a compelling proposition.

**SAP Enterprise Design Premium**
Product Designers who bring enterprise UX specialization — particularly in complex workflow design, data-heavy dashboard visualization, design systems at scale, or accessibility for global enterprise audiences — command a premium at SAP. The Fiori design system serves thousands of applications across SAP's product portfolio, and designers who can contribute to and extend this system have outsized impact.

Designers with experience in AI-augmented interfaces are in particularly high demand as SAP embeds Joule across its product suite. If you can demonstrate expertise in designing conversational AI experiences, intelligent suggestions within workflows, or AI-powered automation interfaces, this directly maps to SAP's highest-priority design initiatives for 2025-2026.
