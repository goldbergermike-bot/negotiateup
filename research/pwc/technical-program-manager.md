**Negotiation DNA**

Technical Program Managers at PwC coordinate complex technology programs — platform migrations, security overhauls, Digital Accelerator product launches, and firm-wide technology transformations. PwC's TPM role involves managing programs that affect 350,000+ employees and thousands of clients across regulated industries. The scale of PwC's technology transformation creates demand for experienced TPMs who can navigate both technical complexity and the firm's partnership culture. Comp is lower base, 10-20% bonus, and limited equity at senior levels. TPMs who deliver high-visibility programs are strong candidates for the partner track.
