**Negotiation DNA**

DevOps Engineers at PwC manage cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and platform reliability for the firm's internal technology and client engagements. PwC's cloud practice is growing as the firm invests in digital transformation capabilities. DevOps engineers support both the firm's internal infrastructure (used by 350,000+ employees) and client-facing cloud consulting engagements. PwC runs across AWS, Azure, and GCP with strategic partnerships with each provider. Comp is lower base, 10-20% bonus, and limited equity at senior levels. The cloud certification programs and training PwC provides through its cloud partnerships are a significant non-financial benefit. [Source: PwC Cloud & Infrastructure Practice 2025-2026]

**Level Mapping:** PwC Associate / Senior Associate (Cloud) = Google L3 SRE = Amazon SDE I (Infra) = Deloitte Consultant (Cloud)

**Global Levers**

1. **Internal Platform Scale:** "I maintain infrastructure used by 350,000+ PwC employees globally. The scale of this internal platform exceeds most tech company DevOps roles."
