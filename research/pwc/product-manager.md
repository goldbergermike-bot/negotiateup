**Negotiation DNA**

Product Managers at PwC drive product strategy for the firm's internal technology platforms, Digital Accelerator products, and client-facing digital solutions. PwC's PM role has growing influence as the firm invests in proprietary technology products — including AI-powered audit tools, tax automation, and consulting enablement platforms. PMs at PwC impact 350,000+ employees and thousands of clients. The comp structure is lower base, 10-20% bonus, and limited equity at senior levels. PMs who can bring product discipline from tech companies are in high demand as PwC builds its product organization. [Source: PwC Technology Product Management 2025-2026]

**Level Mapping:** PwC Manager (PM) = Google L4-L5 PM = Deloitte Manager PM = Accenture Consultant PM
