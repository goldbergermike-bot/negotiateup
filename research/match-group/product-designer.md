The paywall experience, premium feature UI, subscription upgrade flows, and AI feature surfaces I design directly determine conversion rates. A 10% improvement in the Tinder Gold subscription upgrade flow through better UX design, applied to millions of free users, can generate $20M-$40M in incremental annual revenue. My design of AI-powered features — matching explanations, conversation starter UI, compatibility dashboards — determines whether users perceive enough value to subscribe.

By designing AI features that users understand and trust on first interaction, I eliminate costly iterations and A/B test cycles. Well-designed onboarding flows reduce user drop-off by 15-25%, directly reducing customer acquisition cost. My design system work enables faster feature shipping across the entire engineering org.
- **Payback Period:** My design improvements to conversion flows and AI feature surfaces will deliver measurable engagement and conversion lift within my first 2-3 months as A/B tests validate improvements. With a total comp of $175K, even a 5% improvement in premium conversion flow design generates $15M+ in incremental annual revenue — a payback period of approximately 4 days on my annual total comp.

In dating, UX design is the product — the swipe interaction, the profile experience, the conversation interface are what users choose between. My designs for AI-powered features create unique experiences users can't find on Bumble or Grindr, while maintaining distinct brand identities across Tinder, Hinge, Match.com, OkCupid, and PlentyOfFish.
