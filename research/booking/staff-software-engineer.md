Booking.com is replacing its search-based booking interface with AI agents — autonomous systems that handle the entire guest journey through conversation: understanding travel intent, recommending destinations and properties, comparing options, handling booking logistics, and providing in-trip support. This is the shift from 'Search' to 'Agents' — and it means every engineering role at Booking.com now touches revenue-critical guest interactions. "As a Staff Software Engineer, you set the technical vision and architecture for Booking.com's entire AI agent platform — the systems that are replacing search as the primary booking interface. The AI agent IS the booking interface — every agent interaction directly determines whether a guest books, and your platform-level decisions determine how the entire agent ecosystem performs across billions of dollars in transactions. Replacing search with agents means Booking.com's entire $150B+ gross booking volume flows through AI agent interactions built on platforms you design. Compare to: staff search engineers optimize platform-level search infrastructure — staff agent engineers define the platform through which every booking transaction flows as a live, autonomous conversation."
