This is Walmart Global Tech's signature engineering role. Retail AI Platform Engineers build the ML infrastructure and AI systems that power the world's largest retailer — demand forecasting for 240M+ weekly customers, dynamic pricing across 100K+ SKUs, inventory optimization for 4,700+ US stores, supply chain intelligence, and personalized commerce. Walmart processes more retail transactions than any company on Earth, and the AI platform you build directly determines pricing efficiency, inventory availability, and supply chain cost — each optimization point worth hundreds of millions in annual revenue. The 5-15% AI platform premium reflects the rarity of engineers who can build production-grade ML systems at Walmart's unique scale. Walmart Global Tech's compensation is surprisingly competitive — WMT RSUs are blue-chip (stable $600B+ market cap, consistent dividend), base salaries have increased significantly to compete for Bay Area talent, and the 10-15% bonus adds meaningful guaranteed comp.
