#### Negotiation DNA

Data Engineers at Renaissance Technologies build and maintain the data infrastructure that feeds the Medallion Fund's quantitative models — the gold standard of quant performance with approximately 66% annual returns before fees. At the most secretive and successful quant fund in history, founded by mathematician Jim Simons, the data engineering function is mission-critical: the quality, completeness, and timeliness of data directly determines the quality of trading signals. Data engineers design petabyte-scale pipelines that ingest, clean, normalize, and serve financial market data, alternative data sources, and proprietary datasets to the firm's research teams. The deeply academic culture means that data engineers collaborate closely with PhD researchers who have exacting standards for data quality — a missing tick or an incorrect adjustment factor can invalidate months of research. Base salaries range from $260K-$315K, with total compensation of $425K-$615K through bonuses and Medallion Fund access. Data engineers who build reliable, high-throughput data infrastructure become indispensable to the research process and earn increasingly valuable Medallion allocations over time.

#### Medallion Fund Access — The Gold Standard of Quant Performance

The Medallion Fund is the single most valuable negotiation lever in quantitative finance. With approximately 66% annual returns before fees — a track record unmatched by any fund in history — access to the Medallion Fund transforms standard compensation into extraordinary wealth accumulation. The fund is closed to outside investors and available only to current and select former employees, making it a perk that cannot be replicated at any competing firm.

For a Data Engineer, Medallion Fund access is a significant wealth multiplier that reflects the foundational importance of data quality to fund performance. Allocations of $250K-$400K are realistic for data engineers who build and maintain critical data pipelines, generating $75K-$160K in annual investment returns at the fund's historical 30-40% net return rate. Over a 5-year tenure, cumulative Medallion gains can exceed $600K-$1.1M beyond salary and bonus.

Data engineers have a strong case for Medallion access: the Medallion Fund's returns are driven by quantitative models, and those models are only as good as their data. A data engineer who improves data quality, reduces pipeline latency, or integrates a novel alternative data source can have a measurable impact on signal quality and, by extension, fund returns. Candidates should frame their negotiation around the data-to-alpha pipeline, emphasizing that every improvement in data infrastructure compounds into improved research outcomes and fund performance.

#### Global Levers

1. **Lever 1 — Data Infrastructure Criticality:** "The Medallion Fund's quantitative models depend entirely on the quality and timeliness of data pipelines. My experience building petabyte-scale financial data infrastructure at [competitor] — including real-time market data ingestion and alternative data integration — directly impacts fund performance. I need the base to be $305K to reflect this criticality."

2. **Lever 2 — Alternative Data Integration Expertise:** "I have specialized experience integrating alternative data sources — satellite imagery, web scraping pipelines, and NLP-processed news feeds — into quantitative research workflows. This capability is increasingly important to Renaissance's signal discovery process. I am requesting a $140K target bonus and Medallion access within 12 months."

3. **Lever 3 — Competing Data Engineering Offers:** "I have competing offers from Two Sigma at $290K base with significant equity and from a FAANG company at $280K base with $200K in RSUs. The total cash compensation needs to be competitive, especially given the East Setauket location requirement."

4. **Lever 4 — Data Quality and Research Impact:** "Every data quality improvement I make has a multiplicative effect across all of Renaissance's research teams. A single pipeline improvement that reduces data errors by 1% can improve dozens of trading signals. This multiplicative impact justifies a $310K base and a guaranteed $120K first-year bonus, with Medallion access terms that reflect the data-to-alpha pipeline."

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** Open at $310K base with a $135K target bonus and Medallion Fund access within 12 months.
