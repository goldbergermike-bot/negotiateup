#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at VMware/Broadcom

This is the signature engineering role for VMware's cloud-native future. VMware Tanzu encompasses a portfolio of Kubernetes-based products — Tanzu Kubernetes Grid (TKG), Tanzu Application Platform, and Tanzu Mission Control — that enable enterprises to build and run modern applications. Under Broadcom (~$69B acquisition), Tanzu is integrated into VMware Cloud Foundation (VCF), making it central to VMware's strategic product direction.

Tanzu Cloud Engineers work at the intersection of Kubernetes, enterprise infrastructure, and developer experience. They build the platform services that enable enterprises to adopt cloud-native technologies while maintaining the operational controls VMware customers expect. This requires deep Kubernetes expertise, understanding of enterprise requirements, and experience building developer platforms.

The combination of Kubernetes platform engineering skills and enterprise infrastructure understanding is rare. VMware competes for this talent against cloud providers (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) and Kubernetes-native companies (Red Hat/IBM, SUSE, Platform9).
