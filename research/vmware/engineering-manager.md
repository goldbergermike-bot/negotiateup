#### Lever 1: Broadcom Execution Standards

"Broadcom's operating model demands focused execution and efficiency. My track record of driving engineering velocity while maintaining quality aligns with Broadcom's expectations."

#### Lever 2: Enterprise Platform Team Delivery

"I lead teams building infrastructure technology that enterprises depend on for mission-critical workloads. My delivery track record on enterprise platform products provides immediate value."

#### Lever 3: Post-Acquisition Team Stabilization

"Post-acquisition, retaining key talent is critical. My ability to maintain team morale, provide career clarity, and keep delivery velocity high during organizational change preserves engineering capacity."

#### Lever 4: AVGO Equity at Management Level

"Broadcom provides generous management equity. I expect an AVGO RSU package that reflects both team leadership and the strategic importance of the products my team builds."

> **Negotiate Up Strategy**: Open at $210,000 base with AVGO RSUs valued at $85K-$120K/year. Accept-at floor: $300,000 total comp. Emphasize execution track record and post-acquisition leadership.
