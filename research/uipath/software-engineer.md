**Negotiation DNA**
Software Engineers at UiPath build and maintain the enterprise automation platform used by thousands of companies worldwide. As an SWE at UiPath, you contribute to features across the automation suite — Studio (the visual automation designer), Orchestrator (robot management), Robot (the execution engine), or platform infrastructure and APIs. The engineering challenges are distinctive: building software that can interact with virtually any application (desktop, web, mainframe, API) requires understanding a broader range of technology than most SaaS roles.

UiPath's base SWE compensation is competitive within the enterprise automation tier, with total comp of $170K-$270K for US tech hub roles. The company's NYC and Bellevue office locations mean metropolitan cost-of-living applies for on-site roles, and UiPath also supports remote work. The RSU component provides equity upside tied to UiPath's position as the leading enterprise automation platform.

UiPath's engineering culture values creativity, technical quality, and customer impact. The company was founded in Bucharest and maintains a strong engineering presence in Romania, which provides opportunities for global collaboration and an engineering culture that blends Eastern European technical rigor with American product-market thinking. Engineers who enjoy working on diverse technical challenges — from low-level Windows automation to cloud-native AI services — thrive at UiPath.

**UiPath Platform Premium**
Software Engineers who bring experience with .NET/C# (UiPath's primary platform language), Windows desktop automation, or browser automation frameworks have a stack alignment advantage. Additionally, engineers with experience in Python and ML frameworks support UiPath's growing AI features.

Engineers from automation or developer tools companies (JetBrains, Atlassian, GitLab) bring transferable experience with IDE-like development environments, which is relevant to UiPath Studio. If you have experience with visual programming environments, workflow builders, or low-code platforms, these skills are directly applicable.
