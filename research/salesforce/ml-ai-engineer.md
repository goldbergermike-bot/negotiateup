**Negotiation DNA**

The Agentic AI premium (15-30%) applies directly: you're building autonomous agents that generate revenue at $2 per conversation. **Global Levers**

1. **Agentic AI Premium (15-30%):** "I'm building autonomous agents that generate direct consumption revenue. The agentic AI market premium is 15-30% above standard engineering comp, and I expect Salesforce to meet this market rate to compete with AI-native companies."
2. **AI Lab vs. Product Engineering:** Salesforce Research pays research-track comp (competitive with DeepMind/FAIR); Einstein AI product engineering pays product-track comp (competitive with Snowflake Cortex/Databricks ML). Clarify which track and negotiate accordingly.
3. **Foundation Model Experience:** If you have experience training or fine-tuning LLMs, this is the highest-value skill in the Salesforce AI org. Salesforce's proprietary models (xGen, CodeGen) require ML engineers with foundation model expertise.
4. **AI-Native Company Competing Offers:** Competing offers from OpenAI, Anthropic, Cohere, or Mistral create the strongest urgency at Salesforce. Even a verbal indication of an AI-native company process will accelerate the Salesforce offer timeline and push equity higher.

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** "I have competing interest from [AI-native company] at $[X] total comp. Salesforce's Agentforce opportunity is uniquely compelling, but I need the RSU grant at $440K+ over 4 years to justify the move. I also expect the Agentic AI premium to be applied to this role — building autonomous revenue-generating agents is not standard engineering work." Salesforce will counter at $380K-$420K RSUs — this is strong. Push for a sign-on bonus to bridge any remaining gap.
