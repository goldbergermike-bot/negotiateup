**Negotiation DNA**

Product Designers at Tipalti shape how 4,000+ customers — including Twitch, Roblox, GoDaddy, and ZipRecruiter — experience the end-to-end payables automation platform. In B2B fintech, design is not about aesthetics; it is about trust. Every interaction pattern, every data visualization, every workflow you design either builds or erodes confidence in AI-driven financial decisions. The Feb 2026 State of AI in Finance report identifies the AI Trust Gap as the primary barrier to AI adoption in finance, and design is the frontline where that gap is either closed or widened. Finance teams will not adopt AI automation they do not understand, and understanding is a design problem.

As a Trust Architect designer, you are responsible for making AI-driven payables automation feel safe, transparent, and controllable. This means designing explainability interfaces that show finance teams why an AI made a specific payment recommendation, governance dashboards that give CFOs confidence in automated compliance, and training experiences that help users build trust incrementally. Tipalti's $350M+ ARR and pre-IPO positioning mean your design work will be evaluated by public-market investors who care deeply about user adoption metrics and Net Promoter Scores.

Tipalti's private status means equity comes as options on a 4-year vesting schedule, with the current valuation at ~$4-5B. Designers at Tipalti should negotiate for larger base salaries to offset the illiquidity of private equity, while also pushing for options grants that reflect the outsized impact of trust-building design on product adoption and revenue.

### AI Trust — The Trust Architect Premium

**Lever 1 — Trust-Building Design Expertise:** "The Feb 2026 State of AI in Finance report is clear: the AI Trust Gap is fundamentally a user experience problem. **Lever 2 — AI Explainability UX:** "Tipalti's end-to-end payables automation increasingly relies on AI for invoice matching, fraud detection, and payment optimization. But AI is only as valuable as users' willingness to trust it. I design the governance and training interfaces — the explainability layers, the confidence indicators, the human-in-the-loop controls — that close the AI Trust Gap. That specialization commands a premium, and I'd like an options grant of $80K-$100K over four years."

Investors evaluate user adoption metrics, NPS, and feature engagement — all directly shaped by design. That narrative impact justifies a $15K-$20K signing bonus and accelerated first-year vesting."

**Lever 4 — B2B Fintech Design Scarcity:** "Designers who understand both B2B fintech workflows and AI Trust principles are rare.
