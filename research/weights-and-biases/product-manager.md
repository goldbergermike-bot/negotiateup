### AI Development Lifecycle Product Strategy Lever
W&B's 2026 product strategy is expanding from experiment tracking to owning the entire AI development lifecycle — from experimentation through evaluation, deployment, and production monitoring. PMs will define how these product surfaces connect, what the unified user experience looks like, and how W&B positions against Databricks' integrated ML platform. The PM who owns this product strategy directly determines W&B's competitive trajectory.

The LLM evaluation (Weave) product initiative is particularly strategic: it opens a new market segment and establishes W&B in the fastest-growing part of the AI tooling landscape. PMs who can define the evaluation product strategy — what metrics matter, how to compare LLM outputs, how to integrate evaluation into CI/CD — are making decisions that will shape the industry's approach to LLM quality assurance.
