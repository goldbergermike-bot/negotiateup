**Negotiation DNA**

Technical Program Managers at Fiserv orchestrate the cross-functional execution that turns the Clover OS vision into shipped product. With 12% Clover growth and a platform serving 6M+ merchant locations, the complexity of coordinating engineering, product, design, compliance, and operations teams has reached a scale that demands dedicated program management expertise. TPMs at Fiserv own the delivery of programs that span multiple teams, quarters, and sometimes years — ensuring that the SMB Operating Platform transformation stays on track and on budget.

Fiserv's $20B+ revenue base means that program delays have outsized financial consequences. A TPM who keeps a major Clover OS platform initiative on schedule saves the company millions in opportunity costs and prevents competitive ground loss to Stripe, Square, and Toast. The company has adopted High-Margin SaaS pay bands for TPMs because the role requires a rare combination of technical depth, organizational influence, and execution discipline that is essential to platform-scale delivery.

Your negotiation leverage comes from the fact that TPMs are the connective tissue of the organization. Without strong program management, even the best engineering teams ship slowly and inconsistently. At a company undergoing the kind of strategic transformation Fiserv is executing with Clover OS, TPMs are indispensable.
