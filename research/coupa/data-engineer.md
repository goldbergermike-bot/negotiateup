**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at Coupa build and maintain the data infrastructure that processes one of the most valuable datasets in enterprise software: over $6 trillion in cumulative spend data from 3,000+ global enterprises. Since Thoma Bravo acquired Coupa for $8 billion in 2023, data infrastructure has become a first-class investment priority — the entire Autonomous Sourcing vision and AI-powered community intelligence platform depend on data pipelines that are reliable, scalable, and fast. As a Data Engineer, you are building the plumbing that connects raw enterprise procurement data to the AI models that power autonomous sourcing decisions.

The scale of data at Coupa is exceptional. Every procurement transaction, supplier interaction, invoice, expense report, and treasury operation across thousands of enterprise customers flows through data pipelines that you build and maintain. The Supply-Chain Resilience initiative adds real-time data requirements — geopolitical risk signals, tariff changes, supplier health indicators, and market pricing data must be ingested, processed, and made available to downstream models with minimal latency. This is not batch analytics; it is building the real-time data nervous system for global enterprise procurement.

From Coupa's San Mateo hub, Data Engineers collaborate closely with data scientists, ML engineers, and platform engineers to ensure the data infrastructure supports both historical analytics and real-time inference. Under Thoma Bravo's PE ownership, data infrastructure efficiency is closely scrutinized — storage costs, compute optimization, and pipeline reliability all impact the margin metrics that drive exit valuation. Your option grants are tied to this trajectory, and Data Engineers who build efficient, scalable pipelines that enable the AI-powered community intelligence moat are directly contributing to the company's long-term value.
