Western Union competes for design talent against Block, Stripe, Wise, and Coinbase — all of which have strong design cultures and pay at or above market. The USDPT launch urgency means WU needs designers who can ship production-quality financial UX quickly. Candidates with cross-cultural financial product design experience and any exposure to crypto/blockchain UX have significant negotiating leverage.
