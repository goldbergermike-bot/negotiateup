This cross-hub leadership capability is rare and commands a premium.

The EM role sits at the intersection of people leadership and technical strategy — you need managers who understand Solana settlement architecture well enough to make sound technical trade-offs, while also building high-performing distributed teams. That dual capability is your negotiation leverage.
