This is not incremental engineering — this is generational. Legacy competitors use commodity SSDs with generic controllers; Pure Storage builds custom DirectFlash Modules with purpose-built silicon and software that bypasses the entire legacy storage stack.

Pure Storage delivers 20x the efficiency of legacy competitors — this isn't incremental improvement, it's a generation leap.
