1. **Cross-Team Coordination Scope:** "I'm coordinating across engineering, product, design, DS, and operations for initiatives that span the accommodations platform AND Experiences. This dual-marketplace TPM scope — managing dependencies across two distinct product surfaces — exceeds the typical TPM role at a single-product company."

Frame: "I'm driving the program that launches Airbnb's next $5B business. Frame: "I have experience managing AI/ML programs where timelines are uncertain, model performance is iterative, and cross-functional alignment is critical.
