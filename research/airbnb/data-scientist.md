**Negotiation DNA**

Airbnb's data science org is one of the most respected in tech, responsible for innovations like the company's experimentation platform, pricing algorithms, and search ranking models. The no-bonus structure means equity is the key lever. In 2026, the highest-impact DS work is on pricing optimization (dynamic pricing for 7M+ listings), search ranking, demand forecasting, and the new Experiences marketplace analytics — understanding how guests discover, book, and rate curated activities.
