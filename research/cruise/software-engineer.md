**Negotiation DNA**

Cruise Software Engineers build the systems that power autonomous vehicles in San Francisco — from the core autonomy stack (perception, prediction, planning) to simulation infrastructure, fleet management, and rider experience. As a GM subsidiary, Cruise offers competitive Bay Area compensation that must compete directly with Waymo, Google, Meta, and other top-tier employers. The equity component varies — depending on Cruise's current structure, you may receive Cruise-specific equity units or GM stock. The 10-15% target bonus adds meaningful guaranteed comp. Cruise's San Francisco location means high cost of living but also access to the densest concentration of AV talent in the world. The company has navigated regulatory challenges and is focused on demonstrating safe, reliable autonomous transportation at scale. [Source: Cruise SWE Comp Structure 2026]
