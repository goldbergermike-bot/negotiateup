**Negotiation DNA**

Nuvei Corporation, taken private by Advent International in a landmark $6.3 billion deal in 2024, operates as one of the most ambitious payment technology platforms in the global fintech landscape. As a Software Engineer at Nuvei, you are building the infrastructure that powers transactions across 200+ markets with 700+ alternative payment methods, including the company's proprietary Electronic Currency Issuer capabilities that differentiate it from legacy payment processors. The Advent PE backing means the company is in aggressive growth mode with significant capital deployment for talent acquisition, particularly engineers who can scale the platform's i-Gaming vertical leadership and regulated payment corridors.

The recent Pay2All acquisition in Brazil signals Nuvei's expansion strategy into Latin American markets, creating new engineering challenges around localized payment rails, compliance frameworks, and real-time currency conversion at scale. Software Engineers joining now enter during a pivotal PE value-creation phase where Advent International is actively investing in platform modernization, meaning your negotiation leverage is tied to the scarcity of engineers who understand both payment processing and regulated verticals like gaming, crypto, and high-risk merchant categories. Nuvei's e-money license holder status across multiple jurisdictions adds regulatory complexity that commands premium compensation for engineers who can navigate these requirements.

Your negotiation position is further strengthened by Nuvei's Montreal headquarters competing for talent against Toronto and US-based fintech firms, while the company's London and New York offices must match compensation from Stripe, Adyen, and Global Payments. The PE-backed structure means options grants vest over four years with potential for significant upside if Advent achieves its target returns through a future IPO or secondary sale.
