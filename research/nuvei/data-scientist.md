**Negotiation DNA**

Data Scientists at Nuvei sit at the analytical core of a platform that processes payment data across 200+ markets and 700+ alternative payment methods, all backed by Advent International's $6.3 billion investment. The data generated by Nuvei's Electronic Currency Issuer platform, its i-Gaming vertical, and its regulated payment corridors represents one of the most complex and valuable datasets in the fintech industry. Data scientists who can extract actionable intelligence from this data — fraud patterns, conversion optimization insights, risk scoring models, and merchant behavior analytics — directly drive the metrics that Advent tracks for value creation.

The Pay2All acquisition in Brazil has introduced an entirely new data ecosystem that needs integration, normalization, and analysis. Brazilian payment behavior differs significantly from European and North American patterns, and data scientists who can build models that work across these diverse markets while maintaining compliance with e-money license data handling requirements are exceptionally valuable. Nuvei's i-Gaming vertical generates particularly rich datasets — real-time betting transaction flows, player deposit patterns, and withdrawal behavior — that require sophisticated statistical modeling and anomaly detection capabilities.

Your negotiation leverage as a data scientist stems from the direct revenue impact of your work. Fraud detection models that reduce false positives by 1% can save millions in lost transaction volume. Conversion optimization models that identify friction points across Nuvei's 200+ market footprint generate immediate revenue uplift. Options vest over four years under the PE structure, and data scientists who can demonstrate quantifiable impact on Advent's key metrics are well-positioned for accelerated grants and retention packages.
