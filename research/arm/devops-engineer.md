Key points: (1) ARM's royalty model means each engineer's work generates revenue across billions of chips — a multiplier effect no other semiconductor company has. As a DevOps engineer, your infrastructure doesn't support one product; it enables the entire IP development pipeline that generates royalties across billions of chips. (2) Neoverse/CSS commands 3-5x higher royalty rates than mobile cores — meaning your DevOps work supporting Neoverse/CSS development and verification has an outsized revenue-per-design impact. Faster, more reliable Neoverse delivery directly accelerates ARM's highest-value revenue.
