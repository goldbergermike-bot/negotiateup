As a solutions architect, every licensee implementation you enable becomes a stream of royalty revenue across billions of manufactured chips. Your work is literally the conversion point between ARM IP and royalty revenue. (2) Neoverse/CSS commands 3-5x higher royalty rates than mobile cores — meaning your solutions architecture work enabling server and AI compute licensee implementations has an outsized revenue-per-design impact. Every Neoverse design win you support generates 3-5x the royalty of a mobile core win.
