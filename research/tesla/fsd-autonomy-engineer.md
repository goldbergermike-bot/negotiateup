**Negotiation DNA**

This is Tesla's signature engineering role. FSD/Autonomy Engineers build the neural networks, computer vision systems, and planning algorithms that power Tesla's Full Self-Driving software — the technology Elon Musk has called Tesla's "most important product" and the foundation of a future robotaxi fleet worth trillions. Tesla's autonomy stack is unique in the industry: vision-only (no LiDAR), trained on data from millions of Tesla vehicles on the road, and deployed to customers via over-the-air updates. Every line of code you write ships to millions of cars. The 10-20% premium over standard SWE reflects the rarity of autonomy-grade ML talent and the existential importance of FSD to Tesla's market cap thesis. Tesla competes directly with Waymo, Cruise, Aurora, and Mobileye for this talent — and must compensate accordingly. Tesla does not pay annual cash bonuses; total comp is base + RSUs, with RSU value subject to Tesla's famously volatile stock price. [Source: Tesla Autopilot/FSD Team 2026]

**Level Mapping:** Tesla FSD Engineer (Senior) = Waymo Senior SWE = Google L4/L5 Perception = Cruise Senior ML Eng

**What Makes This Role Unique**

The FSD/Autonomy Engineer operates at the intersection of ML research and real-world deployment at unprecedented scale:
- **Vision-Only Autonomy:** Tesla's bet on camera-only autonomy means you're solving harder perception problems than LiDAR-equipped competitors — and the entire industry is watching to see if Tesla's approach wins
- **Fleet-Scale Data Advantage:** You train on billions of miles of real-world driving data from millions of Teslas — a data moat no competitor can replicate
- **End-to-End Neural Networks:** Tesla's shift to end-to-end neural network planning (replacing rules-based systems) means you're at the frontier of applied ML research
- **Dojo Supercomputer:** Tesla built custom AI training hardware (Dojo) specifically for FSD workloads — you have access to compute infrastructure purpose-built for your problem
- **Ship to Millions of Cars:** Your code deploys to real vehicles on real roads via OTA updates — the feedback loop is immediate and the stakes are life-and-death
