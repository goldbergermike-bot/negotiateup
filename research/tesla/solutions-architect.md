**Negotiation DNA**

Solutions Architects at Tesla primarily work on the Tesla Energy and commercial vehicle side of the business — designing Megapack energy storage deployments for utilities and enterprises, architecting Supercharger infrastructure partnerships, supporting Tesla Semi fleet integrations, and building the technical bridge between Tesla's products and enterprise/utility customers. This is Tesla's fastest-growing revenue segment: Tesla Energy (Megapack + Powerwall + Solar) is scaling rapidly, with Megapack deployments growing 100%+ year-over-year for utility-scale energy storage. Solutions Architects are the technical face of Tesla to enterprise customers and energy utilities, translating complex energy infrastructure requirements into Tesla product deployments. No cash bonus; comp is base + RSUs. Unlike pure SaaS solutions architects, Tesla SAs work with physical hardware deployments — you're not configuring cloud services, you're designing multi-megawatt energy storage installations that connect to power grids. [Source: Tesla Energy Solutions Architecture 2026]
