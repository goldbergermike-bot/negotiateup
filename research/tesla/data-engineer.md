**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at Tesla build the data infrastructure that powers every AI system in the company — from the petabytes of driving footage that train FSD neural networks to the energy production and consumption data that optimizes Megapack deployments to the manufacturing telemetry from Gigafactories in Austin, Berlin, and Shanghai. Tesla generates more real-world sensor data than almost any company on Earth: millions of vehicles streaming camera, radar, and telemetry data 24/7. Building data pipelines at this scale is a unique engineering challenge. Tesla's data infrastructure directly enables every ML model, every product decision, and every factory optimization. No cash bonus — comp is base + RSUs. Data engineering at Tesla is not a support function; it's a critical path to every AI product shipping. [Source: Tesla Data Engineering 2026]

**Global Levers**

1. **Petabyte-Scale Data Pipeline Ownership:** "Tesla generates more real-world sensor data than virtually any company. I'll be building and maintaining data pipelines at petabyte scale — this is an order of magnitude beyond typical data engineering roles at SaaS companies. The comp should reflect the scale and complexity."
2. **AI Training Data = FSD Revenue:** "Every FSD model improvement starts with better training data pipelines. My data engineering work directly enables the ML models that power FSD subscriptions and future robotaxi revenue. I'm not building dashboards — I'm building the foundation of Tesla's most valuable product."
3. **Multi-Gigafactory Data Infrastructure:** "Tesla operates Gigafactories on three continents with real-time manufacturing telemetry. My data infrastructure work spans Austin, Fremont, Berlin, and Shanghai — this is global-scale industrial data engineering."
