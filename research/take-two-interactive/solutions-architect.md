**Negotiation DNA**

Solutions Architects at Take-Two design the technical architecture connecting game studios (Rockstar, 2K, Private Division) with centralized platform services — online infrastructure, data platforms, cloud services, and shared technology. The SA role at Take-Two navigates a unique challenge: each studio has deeply proprietary technology (Rockstar's RAGE engine, 2K's NBA simulation engine) while the company wants to share platform services across studios. SAs must understand game engine architecture, online service design, and the specific technical requirements of each studio without constraining their creative freedom. The NYC headquarters hosts the central technology team, while studio-specific SAs work on-site at Rockstar (NYC, San Diego) or 2K (Novato, Baltimore). [Source: Take-Two Platform Architecture 2025]
