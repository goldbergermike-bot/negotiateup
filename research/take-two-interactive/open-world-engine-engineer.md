**What Makes This Role Unique**

The Open-World Engine Engineer builds technology that defines the state of the art in open-world gaming:
- **World Streaming:** Engineering the seamless world-streaming system that loads massive open worlds without loading screens across diverse hardware
- **NPC Simulation:** Building AI-driven NPC behavior systems that simulate thousands of autonomous characters with realistic behaviors
- **RAGE Rendering Pipeline:** Optimizing the proprietary rendering pipeline for photorealistic visuals at scale — vegetation, weather, lighting, and character rendering
- **Physics & Vehicle Simulation:** Engineering the physics systems for vehicle handling, destruction, and environmental interaction that set the industry standard
