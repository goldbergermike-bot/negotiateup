Engineering Managers at Monday.com operate in one of the most competitive environments in global SaaS. The company's Israeli tech DNA means EMs must drive rapid execution while maintaining engineering quality — competing against Asana, Smartsheet, and Microsoft for both customers and talent. EMs who can build high-performing teams in this competitive culture are highly valued.

Monday.com's dual Tel Aviv/NYC headquarters creates unique management challenges — coordinating teams across time zones, cultures, and regulatory environments requires sophisticated management skills.
