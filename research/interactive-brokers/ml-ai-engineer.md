#### ForecastEx — The Automation-Leverage Premium

- **What is ForecastEx?** ForecastEx is IBKR's Forecast Contracts platform for event-based trading. For ML/AI Engineers, ForecastEx is the most technically compelling opportunity at IBKR: building the probabilistic models that price event contracts, designing ML systems that detect market manipulation in prediction markets, creating NLP pipelines that ingest and analyze real-world event data, and developing the AI infrastructure that makes IBKR's prediction markets platform intelligent at scale. ML/AI engineers working on ForecastEx command the highest Automation-Leverage premiums across all engineering roles.

- **What are Automation-Leverage pay bands?** IBKR recognizes that ML/AI engineers who build autonomous decision-making systems — models that operate in production without human oversight, making millions of decisions per day — warrant premium compensation above standard ML engineering roles. These Automation-Leverage bands sit 10-18% above standard fintech ML/AI compensation. For an ML/AI Engineer, this translates to an additional $27K-$71K in total comp, pushing top-band TC from $392K toward $455K+ when the automation premium is fully applied.

- **How does IBKR's automation-first culture amplify ML/AI engineer value?** At IBKR, ML models don't generate recommendations for humans — they make autonomous decisions at production scale. Risk models automatically adjust margin requirements. Fraud models automatically freeze suspicious accounts. Pricing models automatically set spreads. This level of autonomous ML operation means your work has unmediated, real-time impact on IBKR's operations and revenue. You are not building models for human consumption; you are building the AI systems that run the brokerage.

- **How to position yourself as a force multiplier:** In negotiations, emphasize autonomous ML systems you have deployed in production. Use language like: "At [Previous Company], I built and deployed a [risk/pricing/detection] ML system that processes [X]M predictions per day in production with zero human review, replacing [Y] analysts and improving decision accuracy by [Z]%. The system has operated autonomously for [N] months with no degradation." At IBKR, ML/AI engineers are valued for models that operate autonomously at scale — not for research papers or prototype notebooks.
