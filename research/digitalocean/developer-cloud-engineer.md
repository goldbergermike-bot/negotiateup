#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at DigitalOcean

This is the signature engineering role at DigitalOcean. Developer Cloud Engineers build the products that define DigitalOcean's competitive positioning — a cloud platform that is simpler, more transparent, and more developer-friendly than AWS, Azure, or GCP. The role requires both deep cloud infrastructure expertise and a relentless focus on developer experience.

DigitalOcean (NYSE: DOCN, ~$4B market cap) is headquartered in NYC and operates remote-first. The platform serves millions of developers and SMBs with products including Droplets (compute), DOKS (managed Kubernetes), App Platform (PaaS), Functions (serverless), Spaces (object storage), and managed databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, Redis, MongoDB). Developer Cloud Engineers build these products, the control plane that orchestrates them, and the APIs and tools developers interact with.

The combination of cloud infrastructure engineering and developer experience design is rare. DigitalOcean competes for this talent with both cloud hyperscalers (who offer infrastructure depth) and developer-focused companies (who offer DX focus). Few companies require both at DigitalOcean's level.
