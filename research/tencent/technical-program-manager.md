**Negotiation DNA**

Technical Program Managers at Tencent coordinate programs spanning the company's diverse portfolio: gaming launches involving multiple studios and platforms, WeChat feature rollouts to 1.3B users, Tencent Cloud service deployments, and cross-divisional AI initiatives. US-based TPMs are particularly valued for bringing FAANG-caliber program management discipline to Tencent's international operations, which require coordinating teams across US, China, Singapore, and Europe.

Tencent's TPM function in the US has grown rapidly as the company expands internationally. TPMs must navigate both time zone challenges and cultural differences between US and Chinese engineering teams. This cross-cultural program management capability is rare and commands premium compensation.

**Level Mapping:** TPM at Tencent (T10-T11) = L5-L6 TPM at Google, IC5-IC6 TPM at Meta, Sr TPM at Amazon

**The Cross-Border Coordination Premium**

Tencent's global operations require TPMs who can drive alignment across US and Chinese engineering teams operating in different time zones, languages, and cultural contexts. TPMs with this cross-border coordination experience are exceptionally scarce.
