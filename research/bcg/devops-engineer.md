**Negotiation DNA**

DevOps Engineers at BCG manage the cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and platform reliability for both BCG X client deployments and the firm's internal technology. BCG X ships production software to Fortune 500 clients, which means DevOps engineers are responsible for enterprise-grade deployment pipelines, multi-cloud infrastructure, and production reliability. BCG runs primarily on AWS and GCP, with significant Kubernetes adoption for BCG X workloads. BCG does not offer equity; comp is base + performance bonus (25-45%). DevOps engineers who support client-facing deployments tend to earn higher bonuses because their work directly enables billable engagements. [Source: BCG X Platform Engineering 2025-2026]
