#### Negotiation DNA

Solutions Architects at Ally Financial design the technical blueprints that hold together a $196B+ digital-only bank. While Solutions Architects at traditional financial institutions design systems that integrate with branch networks, ATM infrastructure, and physical document workflows, Ally's architects design for a purely digital operating model. Every system a Solutions Architect designs at Ally must work end-to-end without physical fallbacks — from customer onboarding to loan origination to dispute resolution. This all-digital architecture challenge is fundamentally more complex than hybrid digital-physical system design.

Ally's investment in the Ally.ai platform and Responsible AI Institute membership means Solutions Architects must integrate responsible AI principles into enterprise-level system designs. This is not a matter of adding an AI governance layer after the fact — it requires designing systems where fairness, transparency, and explainability are architectural properties, not bolted-on features. When a Solutions Architect designs an auto lending workflow on Ally.ai, they must ensure that every integration point maintains the chain of fairness from data ingestion through model scoring through customer communication.

The role bridges business strategy and technical implementation, and at Ally, that bridge carries unusually heavy traffic. CEO Michael Rhodes' digital-first strategy means every business initiative is a technology initiative — Solutions Architects translate business vision directly into architectural reality with no branch operations layer to absorb complexity.
