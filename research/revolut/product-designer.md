#### :rocket: Revolut US IPO Alpha & IPO Readiness Lever

Revolut's **$9B revenue** and **$3.5B profit** targets are achieved through user engagement — and user engagement is a design problem. The **$75B secondary valuation** reflects not just Revolut's technology but its brand, UX, and the premium experience that justifies Revolut's paid tiers (Plus, Premium, Metal, Ultra).

**What this means for Product Designers:**
- Premium subscription tiers (driving significant per-user revenue) convert or churn based on perceived value — which is fundamentally a design challenge. Your work on upgrade flows, feature discoverability, and premium UX directly impacts ARPU.
- Pre-IPO options at today's strike price give you exposure to the same $75B+ upside as engineers and PMs. Push for equity parity — the app's design quality is a key investor narrative.
- The IPO S-1 will highlight user growth, engagement, and premium conversion as key metrics. These are all design-influenced outcomes.

**IPO Readiness Positioning**: Position yourself as a designer who understands the business impact of UX decisions. During the IPO Readiness phase, Revolut needs designers who can quantify the revenue impact of design changes — A/B test results, conversion improvements, retention lifts. This data-driven design approach directly supports the IPO narrative.

#### Global Levers

**Lever 1: Design-Revenue Impact Parity**
> "Design at a consumer super-app like Revolut has direct revenue impact — every upgrade flow, every onboarding screen, every feature discovery moment drives the $9B target. I'd like to discuss an option grant that reflects this impact, at parity with the PM and engineering equity bands rather than the traditionally lower design band."

**Lever 2: Super-App Design Complexity Premium**
> "Designing for Revolut's super-app — banking, crypto, trading, insurance, travel, business — requires a breadth of domain expertise that exceeds any single-product fintech. This complexity justifies compensation at the top of the design band. I'm not designing one product; I'm designing a financial operating system for 40M+ users."

**Lever 3: Portfolio Leverage — Brand and Conversion Impact**
> "In my portfolio, I can demonstrate a track record of [X% conversion improvement / Y% retention lift / Z% increase in premium subscriptions] at my previous company. At Revolut's scale of 40M+ users, even a 1% conversion improvement on premium upgrades translates to millions in annual revenue. I'd like the equity grant to reflect this measurable impact potential."

**Lever 4: Signing Bonus for Transition Costs**
> "I'm transitioning from a role with liquid equity / a higher base. A signing bonus of £10,000-£15,000 would bridge the first-year gap and allow me to commit fully to the IPO Readiness design challenges without financial friction."
