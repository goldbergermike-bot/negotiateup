#### Global Levers

1. **Quantitative Data Visualization Expertise:** "I specialize in designing interfaces for complex quantitative data — real-time dashboards, multi-dimensional visualizations, and high-density information displays. This expertise directly maps to Jane Street's need for trading and risk interfaces that enable split-second decision-making. Designers with this specialization are extremely rare, and I expect compensation that reflects this scarcity."

2. **Competing Offers from Finance and Big Tech Design Teams:** "I'm evaluating a Senior Product Designer offer from [Google/Apple] at $400K total comp including equity and a design role at Citadel at $375K total comp. Given Jane Street's all-cash structure and 63% compensation surge, I'd expect a total comp of $400K+ in cash, which is more valuable than any RSU-heavy package."

3. **Trader and Researcher Productivity Impact:** "Every design decision I make affects the cognitive load on Jane Street's traders and researchers. A well-designed interface that reduces decision time by 500ms across 1,000 daily trades translates to measurable alpha. I'd like my compensation to reflect this direct impact on trading performance."

4. **Design Systems & Scalable Platform UX:** "I have experience building design systems that scale across multiple internal products — from component libraries to interaction patterns to accessibility frameworks. At Jane Street, a cohesive design system across trading platforms, research tools, and operations dashboards would be a significant infrastructure investment. I'd like a guaranteed first-year bonus of $200K+ to reflect the platform-level impact of this work."

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** Anchor your ask at $200K base and $225K bonus ($425K total comp). Present competing offers from Big Tech design roles ($375K+ including equity) or Citadel ($350K+ total comp). Your walk-away floor should be $175K base and $175K bonus ($350K total comp) — below this, the offer fails to reflect the Alpha Architect premium for design talent at a firm that invests $600K+ per head. For candidates with quantitative visualization or financial platform design experience, push for $225K base with a guaranteed first-year bonus of $200K. Accept at $175K+ base and $175K+ bonus.
