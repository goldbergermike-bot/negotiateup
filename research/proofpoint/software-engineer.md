#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Proofpoint

Proofpoint was acquired by Thoma Bravo for $12.1 billion in 2021, making it one of the largest cybersecurity take-private deals in history. As a private company under Thoma Bravo ownership, Proofpoint operates with a mandate to grow aggressively while maintaining profitability. Software Engineers build the core platform that processes billions of emails daily, applying machine learning models, sandboxing, and behavioral analysis to detect threats in real time.

Proofpoint is headquartered in Sunnyvale, CA and is the undisputed leader in email security, protecting over 80% of Fortune 100 companies. The company's product suite spans email security (Proofpoint Email Protection), threat intelligence, security awareness training, information protection, and cloud security. Engineers work on high-throughput distributed systems that must maintain near-zero false-negative rates for threat detection.

As a private company, Proofpoint competes for talent against public cybersecurity firms (CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, Zscaler) and Big Tech. The lack of publicly traded RSUs means candidates should negotiate aggressively on base salary and bonus, while understanding the potential upside of equity if Thoma Bravo pursues an IPO or secondary sale.

Thoma Bravo's $12.1B acquisition signals massive confidence in Proofpoint's growth trajectory. Use this: "Thoma Bravo's $12.1 billion investment in Proofpoint demonstrates conviction in the platform's growth potential. #### Lever 3: Advanced Threat Protection & ML Pipeline

Proofpoint's Targeted Attack Protection (TAP) uses machine learning and sandboxing to detect zero-day threats. Leverage technical expertise: "My experience building ML-powered detection pipelines directly maps to Proofpoint's TAP platform, where false-negative reduction at billion-message scale is the core engineering challenge."

#### Lever 4: Cloud Transformation & Platform Modernization

Proofpoint is migrating legacy on-premise architectures to cloud-native infrastructure. Negotiate with modernization skills: "Proofpoint's cloud transformation requires engineers who can re-architect systems for cloud-native deployment while maintaining the reliability enterprises depend on. My cloud migration experience accelerates this critical initiative."
