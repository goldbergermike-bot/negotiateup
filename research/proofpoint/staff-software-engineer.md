At this level, Proofpoint competes not just with cybersecurity peers but with Staff-level roles at Google, Meta, and Amazon. The specialization in email security — the number-one enterprise attack vector — combined with the scale of Proofpoint's platform means Staff engineers have rare and valuable expertise.
