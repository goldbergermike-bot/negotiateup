#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Proofpoint

ML/AI Engineers at Proofpoint work on one of the most consequential applications of machine learning — detecting threats in real time at massive scale. The models they build must classify billions of messages with near-zero false negatives (a missed threat can compromise an enterprise) while maintaining acceptable false-positive rates (legitimate email must flow). This is an adversarial ML problem where attackers actively try to evade detection.

Proofpoint's $12.1B valuation under Thoma Bravo is substantially built on its ML-powered detection capabilities. The company's Nexus AI models, URL defense systems, and attachment sandboxing all rely on ML engineers. Competition for ML talent is the most intense segment of engineering hiring, and Proofpoint must compete with Big Tech AI labs and well-funded AI startups.
