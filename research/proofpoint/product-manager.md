#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Proofpoint

Product Managers at Proofpoint own product strategy for security tools that protect the largest enterprises in the world. The PM role requires deep understanding of the threat landscape, enterprise buyer needs, and technical platform capabilities. Under Thoma Bravo's $12.1B ownership, PMs are expected to drive both product innovation and revenue growth.

Proofpoint PMs work across Email Protection, Targeted Attack Protection (TAP), security awareness training, information protection, and cloud security. The breadth of the product portfolio and the enterprise customer base create a PM role with significant strategic scope and business impact.
