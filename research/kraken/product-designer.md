#### Negotiation DNA

The Product Designer at Kraken shapes the user experience of a crypto exchange that serves millions of retail and institutional traders worldwide. Designers at Kraken own end-to-end product experiences — from trading interfaces and portfolio dashboards to onboarding flows and compliance screens. With Kraken's Q1/Q2 2026 IPO filing at a $20B Citadel-backed valuation, product designers are IPO-critical because the user experience directly impacts retention, engagement, and the conversion metrics investors evaluate. Kraken's design challenges are uniquely complex: balancing simplicity for retail users with the data density and precision required by institutional traders, all within a heavily regulated financial environment. The pre-IPO equity window makes this a strong moment for designers to negotiate, as UX improvements during the IPO runway directly translate to user growth metrics.

#### IPO Window — The 20% Pre-Listing Share Negotiation

Kraken filed for its IPO in Q1/Q2 2026 with a Citadel-backed $20B valuation. For product designers, the pre-IPO window is an opportunity to capture equity upside that directly ties to the user experience improvements they ship. Public-market investors evaluate user engagement and retention metrics — both of which are heavily influenced by design quality.

**Why 20% matters for Product Designers:**
- A standard designer grant of $170K/4yr in options could appreciate to $289K-$425K+ at a post-IPO $30B-$40B market cap
- Design-driven UX improvements during the IPO runway directly impact user growth and engagement metrics presented to investors
- Post-IPO designer hires will receive RSUs at market price; 20% more shares now captures the pre-listing discount
- Designers at Coinbase who joined pre-IPO saw 3-5x appreciation on initial option grants
- Kraken's push into institutional trading and new product verticals creates outsized design impact opportunities

**Negotiation script:**
> "As a product designer, I'll be directly shaping the user experience that drives the engagement and retention metrics Kraken presents to public-market investors. The quality of the trading interface and user flows I design will influence how investors perceive Kraken's product-market fit. Given my contribution to these IPO-critical metrics and the illiquidity risk of pre-IPO options, I'm requesting a 20% increase in my options grant."
