#### Negotiation DNA

The Data Engineer at Kraken builds and operates the data infrastructure that powers every analytical, compliance, and operational system on a crypto exchange processing billions in daily trading volume. Data engineers at Kraken own real-time streaming pipelines (Kafka, Flink), data warehouse architecture (Snowflake, BigQuery), blockchain data ingestion across multiple chains, regulatory reporting data flows, and the foundational data platform that data scientists, analysts, product teams, and compliance officers depend on. With Kraken's Q1/Q2 2026 IPO filing at a $20B Citadel-backed valuation, data engineers are IPO-critical because the financial reporting infrastructure, regulatory data pipelines, and metrics systems they build are prerequisites for SOX compliance and SEC reporting requirements as a public company. The transition from private to public company demands institutional-grade data governance, auditability, and accuracy — all of which flow through the systems data engineers build. The pre-IPO equity window makes this an ideal moment to negotiate aggressively, as the data infrastructure built now will serve as the backbone of Kraken's public-company reporting for years to come.

#### IPO Window — The 20% Pre-Listing Share Negotiation

Kraken filed for its IPO in Q1/Q2 2026 with a Citadel-backed $20B valuation. Data engineers are foundational to the IPO process because every financial metric, regulatory report, and compliance disclosure in the S-1 filing depends on the data pipelines and infrastructure they build. SOX compliance — a hard requirement for public companies — demands auditable, accurate, and reproducible data flows. Data engineers are the ones who make this possible.

**Why 20% matters for Data Engineers:**
- A standard data engineer grant of $340K/4yr in options could appreciate to $578K-$850K+ at a post-IPO $30B-$40B market cap
- SOX compliance requires institutional-grade data infrastructure — the pipelines built by data engineers during the IPO runway become the foundation of all public-company financial reporting
- Post-IPO data engineer hires will receive RSUs at market price; 20% more shares now captures the full pre-listing discount
- Data engineers at Coinbase who joined pre-IPO saw 3-6x option appreciation from 409A to peak post-listing value
- Kraken's multi-chain blockchain data ingestion, real-time trading analytics, and regulatory reporting pipelines represent uniquely complex data engineering challenges that attract a thin talent pool
