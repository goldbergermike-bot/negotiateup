#### Negotiation DNA

The Product Manager at Kraken defines the user experience and feature roadmap for one of the world's largest crypto exchanges. PMs at Kraken own product strategy across trading interfaces, staking products, derivatives platforms, and institutional onboarding flows. With Kraken's Q1/Q2 2026 IPO filing at a $20B Citadel-backed valuation, product managers are IPO-critical because the features they ship — and the metrics they drive — will be scrutinized by public-market investors evaluating user growth, engagement, and monetization. Kraken's PM role requires a rare blend of financial product expertise, regulatory awareness, and crypto-native user empathy. The pre-IPO window makes this an ideal moment for PMs to negotiate aggressively on options, as the products they build will directly influence the company's post-listing valuation trajectory.

#### IPO Window — The 20% Pre-Listing Share Negotiation

Kraken filed for its IPO in Q1/Q2 2026 with a Citadel-backed $20B valuation. Product managers are uniquely positioned during the pre-IPO window because the products they ship directly determine the growth metrics that public-market investors use to value the company. User engagement, revenue per user, and product-market fit narratives all flow through PM decisions.

**Why 20% matters for Product Managers:**
- A standard PM grant of $300K/4yr in options could appreciate to $510K-$750K+ at a post-IPO $30B-$40B market cap
- PMs who define the product strategy during the IPO runway create the narrative that investors buy into — this is the highest-leverage moment for PM impact
- Post-IPO PM hires will receive RSUs at market price without the pre-listing discount; 20% more shares now captures this entire arbitrage
- Product managers at Coinbase who joined pre-IPO saw their options appreciate 3-5x from 409A to peak post-listing value
- Kraken's product roadmap — including institutional derivatives, staking expansion, and DeFi integration — positions PMs to drive significant revenue growth

**Negotiation script:**
> "Product managers define the features and metrics that will be highlighted in Kraken's S-1 filing and investor roadshow. I'll be shaping the product narrative that directly influences how public-market investors value the company. Given this outsized impact and the liquidity risk of pre-IPO options, I'm requesting a 20% increase in my options grant to reflect my contribution to the IPO valuation story."

1. **Pre-IPO Equity Lever:** "I'm joining during the pre-IPO window when product decisions have the highest impact on company valuation. The features I ship in the next 12-18 months will directly shape investor perception. I'm asking for 20% more options to align my compensation with the strategic timing of my contribution."

2. **Competing Offer Lever:** "I have a competing offer from Robinhood at $340K TC with liquid RSUs. Kraken's pre-IPO options carry meaningful illiquidity risk, and I need the grant increased to $350K/4yr with a $30K signing bonus to make the switch financially rational."

PMs with this domain expertise are exceptionally scarce — fewer than 500 people globally have shipped crypto exchange products at scale. This scarcity should be reflected in both base and equity."

4. **Metrics Ownership Lever:** "I'll own the product KPIs that Kraken reports to public-market investors post-IPO — MAUs, trading volume, revenue per user, and retention. Ownership of these investor-facing metrics commands a premium because my decisions directly impact the company's public valuation. I'd like this reflected in a $20K increase in base and 20% more options."
