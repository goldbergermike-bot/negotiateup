**Negotiation DNA**

ML/AI Engineers at Alibaba work on some of the world's largest-scale AI deployment challenges.

US-based AI engineers typically work on AI infrastructure for Alibaba Cloud (PAI platform), international AI research, or foundational model development. Compensation is competitive with FAANG AI roles, and Alibaba is authorized to match offers from frontier AI labs for senior AI talent. DAMO Academy researchers publish extensively and have contributed significant open-source AI frameworks.
