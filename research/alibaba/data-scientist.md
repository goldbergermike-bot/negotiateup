**Negotiation DNA**

Data Scientists at Alibaba analyze user behavior across the world's largest e-commerce ecosystem. With 1B+ annual active consumers on Taobao and Tmall, Alibaba generates commerce behavioral data at a scale no other company can match. Data scientists optimize search ranking, product recommendations, pricing algorithms, supply chain forecasting, and advertising effectiveness -- each improvement directly impacting billions in GMV.

US-based data scientists typically focus on international commerce analytics, Alibaba Cloud customer analytics, and AI/ML research. Compensation is competitive with FAANG data science roles. In China, data scientists at Alibaba (typically P6-P8) are among the highest-paid analytics professionals, with strong performance bonuses tied to business impact.

**Level Mapping:** Data Scientist at Alibaba (P6-P7) = L4-L5 at Google, IC4-IC5 at Meta, DS Senior at Amazon, equivalent Netflix/Spotify

**The Commerce Optimization Premium**

Alibaba's data science challenges are uniquely impactful: a 0.1% improvement in search conversion across Taobao can generate hundreds of millions in additional GMV. Data scientists with experience in search ranking, recommendation systems, or marketplace optimization have exceptional leverage.
