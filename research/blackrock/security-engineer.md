#### Negotiation DNA

Security Engineers at BlackRock protect the most critical financial technology infrastructure on the planet. With $11.5 trillion in AUM and Aladdin processing risk analytics for $21T+ in total assets, the security posture of BlackRock's technology stack is a matter of systemic financial stability. BlackRock (NYSE: BLK) faces threat actors ranging from nation-state adversaries to sophisticated cybercriminal organizations — all targeting the world's largest asset manager and its interconnected client ecosystem of 200+ institutional investors. The $5-8 trillion AI capex theme introduces entirely new attack surfaces — AI model poisoning, adversarial ML, prompt injection, data exfiltration through AI interfaces — that demand security engineers with both traditional cybersecurity expertise and emerging AI security knowledge. Candidates should negotiate for "Active Alpha" premiums by framing their security work as existential risk mitigation: a single major breach at BlackRock's scale could trigger cascading financial market consequences. Security engineers who combine financial services regulatory expertise (SOC 2, SOX, GDPR, SEC cybersecurity rules) with AI security knowledge are among the scarcest and most valuable hires in the industry.

#### Aladdin AI — The Active Alpha Premium

BlackRock is deploying Aladdin AI across its entire $11.5T+ AUM ecosystem, and Security Engineers are the guardians ensuring this AI transformation doesn't introduce catastrophic risk. As Aladdin AI processes natural language queries against portfolio data, generates predictive risk models, and automates investment workflows, every new AI capability creates new attack vectors that must be secured before deployment.

**Why this matters for your negotiation:**
- Security Engineers who specialize in AI security and financial infrastructure command a **15-20% premium** over standard VP/Director-band offers — you protect the platform that manages $21T+ in global assets
- A single security incident at BlackRock could cascade across global financial markets, trigger regulatory enforcement, and destroy billions in enterprise value — your work is existential risk mitigation
- The $5-8 trillion AI capex theme introduces novel security challenges — adversarial ML, AI model governance, prompt injection, data poisoning — that require entirely new security expertise
- BlackRock competes with Google Security, CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, and top financial institutions for security talent — and the talent pool with financial services + AI security experience is vanishingly small
