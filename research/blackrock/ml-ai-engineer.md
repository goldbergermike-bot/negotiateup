#### Negotiation DNA

ML/AI Engineers at BlackRock are the builders of the intelligence layer that powers the most consequential investment platform in the world. With $11.5 trillion in AUM and Aladdin processing analytics for $21T+ in total assets, your models, training pipelines, and inference systems directly influence how the world's largest institutional investors make decisions worth trillions. BlackRock (NYSE: BLK) is in an all-out war for ML/AI talent — competing with Google DeepMind, OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta FAIR, Citadel, and Two Sigma for engineers who can build production ML systems at financial-data scale. The $5-8 trillion AI capex theme has made this the most strategically important hire at BlackRock, and CEO Larry Fink has repeatedly emphasized in public filings and shareholder letters that AI is BlackRock's primary competitive moat going forward. Candidates should demand "Active Alpha" premiums that reflect the +15-25% AI Premium the market commands for ML engineers with production experience. This role sits at the intersection of the most valuable skillset (ML/AI engineering) and the most consequential application domain (global asset management) — price yourself accordingly.

#### Aladdin AI — The Active Alpha Premium

BlackRock is deploying Aladdin AI across its entire $11.5T+ AUM ecosystem, and ML/AI Engineers are the architects and builders of the AI capabilities that define Aladdin's next generation. This is not supporting work — this is the core. You build the models that generate alpha signals, the NLP systems that parse earnings calls and research, the risk prediction engines that anticipate market dislocations, and the recommendation systems that power portfolio construction for the world's largest investors.

**Why this matters for your negotiation:**
- ML/AI Engineers at BlackRock command a **+15-25% AI Premium** over standard engineering offers — this premium is market-wide and reflects the extreme scarcity of production ML talent
- Your models directly generate investment alpha — measurable outperformance that justifies BlackRock's management fees across $11.5T in AUM; even a fraction of a basis point improvement at this scale is worth hundreds of millions
- BlackRock is in direct competition with OpenAI ($300K+ ML engineer comp), Google DeepMind ($400K+ researcher comp), Citadel ($500K+ quant comp), and Two Sigma ($450K+ researcher comp) — your offers from these firms are your most powerful negotiation lever
- Aladdin AI's $1.5B+ technology revenue depends on the quality of ML capabilities — your work is the product differentiation

**Leverage language:** "As an ML/AI Engineer building Aladdin AI's core intelligence layer, my models directly generate investment alpha across $11.5T in AUM. The market for production ML engineers with financial domain expertise commands a 15-25% premium, and my competing offers from [OpenAI / Google DeepMind / Citadel / Two Sigma] confirm this valuation. I expect an Active Alpha premium that reflects both the AI talent market and the direct alpha-generation impact of my work."

#### Global Levers

1. **The Alpha-Generation Lever:** "My ML models don't just process data — they generate investment alpha. At BlackRock's scale, a single basis point of improved risk-adjusted return across $11.5T in AUM translates to over $1 billion in annual value. My compensation should reflect the alpha-generation potential of my work, not generic ML engineer market rates. I'm targeting $290K+ base with $140K+/yr in RSUs."
