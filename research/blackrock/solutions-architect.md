#### Negotiation DNA

Solutions Architects at BlackRock are the strategic technologists who design how Aladdin integrates with the world's most sophisticated institutional investors. With $11.5 trillion in AUM and 200+ external clients on the Aladdin platform, Solutions Architects own the technical relationship between BlackRock's technology and the investment workflows of sovereign wealth funds, pension systems, and global asset managers. BlackRock (NYSE: BLK) positions Solutions Architects at the Director band, reflecting the role's dual requirement of deep technical expertise and client-facing business acumen. The $5-8 trillion AI capex theme has elevated the importance of this role — as Aladdin AI capabilities are deployed across the platform, Solutions Architects must translate complex AI features into actionable integration architectures for clients with varying technical maturity. Candidates should negotiate for "Active Alpha" premiums by emphasizing their direct influence on Aladdin's $1.5B+ technology revenue through client architecture design, implementation success, and platform adoption. This role is uniquely difficult to hire for because it requires the intersection of enterprise architecture expertise, financial domain knowledge, and client relationship skills.

#### Aladdin AI — The Active Alpha Premium

BlackRock is deploying Aladdin AI across its entire $11.5T+ AUM ecosystem, and Solutions Architects are the architects of AI adoption for institutional clients. Your technical designs determine how 200+ of the world's largest investors integrate Aladdin AI capabilities — natural language queries, predictive risk models, and AI-driven portfolio construction — into their existing investment workflows.

**Why this matters for your negotiation:**
- Solutions Architects who design Aladdin AI integrations command a **12-18% premium** over standard Director-band offers — you directly control the adoption velocity of BlackRock's highest-revenue product initiative
- Every successful Aladdin AI client implementation you architect drives incremental technology revenue measured in millions per client annually
- The $5-8 trillion AI capex theme means institutional clients are demanding AI capabilities from their platform providers — your architecture work determines whether BlackRock or competitors capture this demand
- Solutions Architects with AI integration experience and financial domain expertise are among the hardest roles for BlackRock to fill externally

**Leverage language:** "As a Solutions Architect designing Aladdin AI integrations for institutional clients, I directly influence the adoption and revenue trajectory of BlackRock's $1.5B+ technology platform. My experience in [enterprise architecture / AI system integration / financial technology consulting / client-facing technical leadership] positions me to accelerate Aladdin AI's client deployment pipeline. I expect an Active Alpha premium that reflects my direct revenue impact."

#### Global Levers

1. **The Revenue Attribution Lever:** "Every Aladdin client integration I architect generates millions in recurring technology revenue. My track record of [successful enterprise integrations / client retention through technical excellence / platform adoption acceleration] has direct, measurable revenue impact. I'm targeting $245K+ base with $90K+/yr in RSUs to reflect this attribution."

2. **The Consulting Firm Lever:** "I'm currently considering offers from [McKinsey Digital / Deloitte Cloud / Accenture AI] at $X total comp, plus [AWS / Google Cloud / Microsoft] Solutions Architect roles at competitive packages. BlackRock's Aladdin platform is uniquely compelling, but I need the economics to reflect the revenue-generating nature of this role versus a cost-center consulting position."

3. **The Aladdin AI Adoption Lever:** "Aladdin AI's success depends on institutional clients actually adopting and integrating AI capabilities into their workflows. I'm the architect who makes that happen. Without effective solutions architecture, BlackRock's AI investment doesn't translate to revenue. My compensation should reflect this critical adoption multiplier."

4. **The MD-Track Lever:** "Solutions Architects at the Director level who drive major client platform adoptions are on the Managing Director track. If BlackRock can structure a package with MD-track visibility, a $40K-$55K signing bonus, and a compensation review tied to Aladdin AI client deployment milestones at 18 months, I'll commit fully and decline competing processes."

> **Negotiate Up Strategy:** Target $245K-$248K base with $90K-$97K/yr in RSUs.
