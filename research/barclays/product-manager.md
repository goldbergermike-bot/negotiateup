**Negotiation DNA**

Product Managers at Barclays own strategy and execution for banking products spanning UK retail banking, Barclaycard, investment banking platforms, and wealth management. PMs must navigate FCA regulatory requirements, Open Banking mandates, and competition from UK neobanks. The PM role requires balancing digital innovation with regulatory compliance in one of the world's most competitive retail banking markets.

**The UK Banking Product Premium**

UK banking is one of the most competitive retail banking markets globally, with neobanks, Open Banking challengers, and established players all competing for customers. PMs who can drive product innovation while navigating FCA regulatory requirements develop skills that are valued globally.
