#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" is the company's most ambitious product expansion since its founding. In 2026, Coinbase is moving beyond crypto-only trading to offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single unified platform. This expansion dramatically increases Coinbase's total addressable market — from the $2T crypto market to the $100T+ global securities market.

For Security Engineers, the equity story is about risk mitigation as value creation:

- **Attack Surface Expansion:** The Everything Exchange triples Coinbase's threat surface. You are now defending crypto wallets, equities settlement systems, and prediction market resolution engines simultaneously. Cross-asset attack vectors — exploiting the bridge between crypto and equities systems — are an entirely new threat category that does not exist at single-product companies. Your security work directly prevents catastrophic loss events.
- **$2B Buyback Protection:** With $200K-$400K in RSUs, the buyback program provides structural protection for your equity. A major security breach could devastate COIN's stock price. Your security engineering is itself a form of equity protection — both for Coinbase and for your personal RSU value.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** The Everything Exchange must meet SEC, CFTC, and state-level security requirements simultaneously. Security Engineers who can architect controls that satisfy multiple regulators are preventing compliance failures that could cost Coinbase its licenses.
- **Negotiation Framing:** Position your ask as "My security work prevents nine-figure loss events. A single breach could cost Coinbase hundreds of millions and destroy COIN's stock price. My RSU grant should reflect the value I protect, not just the code I write."
