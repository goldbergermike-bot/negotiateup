#### Negotiation DNA

The Data Engineer at Coinbase is the architect of the data infrastructure powering the "Everything Exchange" — the 2026 initiative to unify stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single platform. At the IC4-IC5 level, you design and build the data pipelines, warehouses, and real-time streaming systems that ingest, transform, and serve data from three fundamentally different asset classes. The Everything Exchange creates a data engineering challenge unlike anything at a single-product company: you must unify blockchain transaction data, equities market feeds (NASDAQ/NYSE), and prediction market event streams into a coherent data layer that serves analytics, compliance reporting, machine learning models, and real-time trading features. Coinbase (NASDAQ: COIN) compensates Data Engineers with RSU grants on a 4-year vest, and the $2B share buyback program protects your equity. Every product decision at Coinbase depends on the data infrastructure you build.

#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" is the company's most ambitious product expansion since its founding. In 2026, Coinbase is moving beyond crypto-only trading to offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single unified platform. This expansion dramatically increases Coinbase's total addressable market — from the $2T crypto market to the $100T+ global securities market.

For Data Engineers, the equity story is about infrastructure as the foundation:

- **Data Unification Challenge:** You are building the data layer that makes the Everything Exchange possible. Blockchain data, equities market feeds, and prediction market events use different schemas, latency profiles, and data models. Unifying them into a single queryable layer is a foundational engineering challenge — every analyst, data scientist, ML engineer, and product manager at Coinbase depends on your work.
- **$2B Buyback Protection:** With $155K-$330K in RSUs, the buyback program provides structural support for your equity. Coinbase's $2B share repurchase commitment signals institutional confidence in COIN's value.
- **Compliance and Regulatory Data:** The Everything Exchange requires real-time compliance reporting across multiple regulators. Your data pipelines must produce audit trails for the SEC (equities), CFTC (prediction markets), and state regulators (crypto). This compliance data infrastructure is not optional — it is a licensing requirement.
- **Negotiation Framing:** Position your ask as "I'm building the unified data infrastructure that every team at Coinbase depends on — from analytics to compliance to machine learning. My RSU grant should reflect the foundational nature of this work."
