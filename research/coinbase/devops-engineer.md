#### Negotiation DNA

The DevOps Engineer at Coinbase is the infrastructure backbone of the "Everything Exchange" — the 2026 initiative to unify stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single platform. At the IC4-IC5 level, you own the CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code, container orchestration, and deployment automation that enables Coinbase engineers to ship multi-asset trading features reliably and at velocity. The Everything Exchange dramatically increases infrastructure complexity: you are managing deployments across systems that must handle real-time crypto settlement, T+1 equities clearing, and event-driven prediction market resolution — each with different uptime requirements, latency tolerances, and regulatory audit needs. Coinbase (NASDAQ: COIN) compensates DevOps Engineers with RSU grants on a 4-year vest, and the $2B share buyback program protects your equity. If Coinbase's infrastructure fails during a market event, the financial impact is measured in millions — your reliability engineering directly protects Coinbase's revenue and reputation.

#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" is the company's most ambitious product expansion since its founding. In 2026, Coinbase is moving beyond crypto-only trading to offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single unified platform. This expansion dramatically increases Coinbase's total addressable market — from the $2T crypto market to the $100T+ global securities market.

For DevOps Engineers, the equity story is about infrastructure as revenue protection:

- **Uptime = Revenue:** In a trading platform, downtime means lost trades, lost fees, and lost users. The Everything Exchange triples the surface area of systems that must stay online. Your infrastructure work directly protects Coinbase's revenue stream across three asset classes. This revenue-protection role justifies premium RSU grants.
- **$2B Buyback Protection:** With $120K-$280K in RSUs, the buyback program provides a price floor for your equity. Coinbase is committing $2B to share repurchases, signaling confidence in COIN's value and protecting your compensation.
- **Infrastructure Complexity Premium:** Managing infrastructure for a multi-asset platform is significantly harder than single-product DevOps. You need different deployment strategies for crypto (24/7, zero-downtime), equities (market hours, regulatory windows), and prediction markets (event-driven, burst scaling). This complexity justifies higher compensation.
- **Negotiation Framing:** Frame your ask as "I'm building the infrastructure that ensures the Everything Exchange stays online during the most critical market moments. My RSUs should reflect the revenue I protect, not just the pipelines I build."
