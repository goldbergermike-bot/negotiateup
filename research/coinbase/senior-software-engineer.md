#### Negotiation DNA

The Senior Software Engineer at Coinbase is the technical backbone of the "Everything Exchange" — the 2026 initiative that transforms Coinbase from a crypto exchange into a unified multi-asset trading platform spanning stocks, crypto, and prediction markets. At the IC5 level, you own system design decisions, mentor junior engineers, and drive architecture choices that will determine whether the Everything Exchange can handle equities settlement, prediction market resolution, and crypto trading on a single backend. Coinbase (NASDAQ: COIN) compensates at the IC5 level with significant RSU grants on a 4-year vest, and the $2B share buyback program ensures your equity has structural downside protection. This is the level where Coinbase expects you to shape technical direction — and your comp should reflect that influence.

#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" represents the most significant product expansion in the company's history. By 2026, Coinbase will offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single platform — moving the company's total addressable market from $2T (crypto only) to over $100T (global securities + derivatives + prediction markets).

For Senior Software Engineers, the equity implications are substantial:

- **Architecture Ownership:** You are designing the systems that enable multi-asset trading. Your technical decisions directly impact whether Coinbase captures equities market share from Robinhood and Schwab, or falls behind. This scope justifies premium RSU grants.
- **$2B Buyback Floor:** Coinbase's $2B share buyback program creates a structural price support for COIN stock. As a Senior SWE with $300K-$560K in RSUs, this buyback program is effectively insurance on your largest compensation component.
- **Revenue Multiplier:** Every new asset class Coinbase adds creates a new revenue stream. Stocks alone could double Coinbase's active user base. Prediction markets add a high-margin, high-engagement product. Your RSUs are tied to a revenue diversification story, not a single-product bet.
- **Negotiation Angle:** Frame your RSU ask as "buyback-protected growth equity in a company entering its most aggressive expansion phase." Request that grant size reflect post-Everything Exchange revenue projections, not trailing crypto-only metrics.
