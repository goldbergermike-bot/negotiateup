#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" is the company's most ambitious product expansion since its founding. In 2026, Coinbase is moving beyond crypto-only trading to offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single unified platform. This expansion dramatically increases Coinbase's total addressable market — from the $2T crypto market to the $100T+ global securities market.

For ML/AI Engineers, the equity story is about intelligence as competitive advantage:

- **Cross-Asset ML Complexity:** You are building models that span three asset classes with fundamentally different statistical properties. Crypto markets are 24/7 with extreme volatility, equities follow market hours with regulated price formation, and prediction markets are event-driven with binary outcomes. Building unified ML systems across these domains is a challenge that does not exist at any other company. Your RSU grant should include the 15-25% AI premium that reflects this scarcity.
- **$2B Buyback Amplification:** With $300K-$560K in RSUs, the buyback program provides substantial protection for your largest compensation component. At AI-premium RSU levels, even a modest buyback-driven price improvement adds significant dollar value to your 4-year grant.
- **Revenue-Generating Models:** Your ML models directly generate revenue. Fraud detection saves millions in loss prevention. Recommendation systems drive engagement and trading volume. Risk scoring enables Coinbase to offer new products. These are not research exercises — they are production systems with measurable revenue impact.
- **Negotiation Framing:** Position your ask as "AI/ML talent is the scarcest resource in fintech. I'm bringing production ML expertise to the most complex multi-asset platform in the industry. My comp should reflect the AI premium, the cross-asset complexity, and the buyback-protected equity."
