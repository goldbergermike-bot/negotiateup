#### Negotiation DNA

The Product Designer at Coinbase is responsible for designing the user experience that will make the "Everything Exchange" feel like one seamless platform — not three bolted-together products. At the D3-D4 level, you own the design of trading flows, portfolio views, order entry, and market data visualization across stocks, crypto, and prediction markets. The design challenge is unique: you need to create an interface that feels intuitive for a crypto trader buying Bitcoin, an equities investor purchasing Apple stock, and a prediction market participant betting on election outcomes — all in the same session. Coinbase (NASDAQ: COIN) compensates designers with RSU grants on a 4-year vest, and the $2B share buyback program protects your equity. If you can unify three asset classes into one coherent experience, you are designing the interface that defines a new product category.

#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" is the company's most ambitious product expansion since its founding. In 2026, Coinbase is moving beyond crypto-only trading to offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single unified platform. This expansion dramatically increases Coinbase's total addressable market — from the $2T crypto market to the $100T+ global securities market.

For Product Designers, the equity story is about user experience as competitive moat:

- **Design as Differentiator:** The Everything Exchange will live or die on user experience. If the unified trading interface feels clunky or confusing, users will stay on Robinhood for stocks and Coinbase for crypto. Your design work is the competitive moat — the reason users choose one platform over two. This makes your contribution directly tied to revenue and user retention.
- **$2B Buyback Protection:** With $100K-$220K in RSUs, the buyback program provides meaningful downside protection. Coinbase is supporting COIN's price with $2B in share repurchases, which means your equity has a structural floor even during market volatility.
- **User Acquisition Impact:** A well-designed Everything Exchange will attract millions of new users who currently trade stocks on Robinhood or Schwab. Your design decisions directly impact Coinbase's user growth metrics, which drive COIN's stock price. Your RSUs benefit from the users your designs attract.
- **Negotiation Framing:** Position your ask as "I'm designing the interface that will determine whether the Everything Exchange succeeds or fails as a product. My RSU grant should reflect the revenue impact of user experience on a multi-asset platform."
