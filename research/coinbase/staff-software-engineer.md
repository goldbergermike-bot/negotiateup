#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" is the company's most ambitious product expansion since its founding. In 2026, Coinbase is moving beyond crypto-only trading to offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single unified platform. This expansion dramatically increases Coinbase's total addressable market — from the $2T crypto market to the $100T+ global securities market.

For Staff Software Engineers, the equity calculus is amplified by scope:

- **Cross-System Ownership:** You are the architect bridging crypto settlement, equities clearing, and prediction market resolution. No other engineer at Coinbase has this breadth. Your RSU grant must reflect that you are designing the connective tissue of the Everything Exchange — the systems that make multi-asset trading possible on one platform.
- **$2B Buyback Amplification:** With $500K-$900K in RSUs, the $2B buyback program is not just protection — it is a value accelerator. Coinbase repurchasing shares at scale reduces dilution and supports the per-share value of your grant. At Staff-level RSU volumes, even a 10% buyback-driven price improvement adds $50K-$90K to your 4-year grant value.
- **Staff Premium Justification:** The 15-25% premium over IC5 compensation reflects the reality that Staff Engineers set technical direction across the entire Everything Exchange. Your architecture decisions determine whether Coinbase can ship multi-asset trading in 2026 or misses the market window. This leverage justifies a grant floor of $500K in RSUs.
- **Negotiation Framing:** Position your RSU ask as "growth equity with buyback protection and Staff-level scope." Demand that your grant size reflect the Everything Exchange TAM expansion, not legacy crypto-only headcount planning.
