#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" is the company's most ambitious product expansion since its founding. In 2026, Coinbase is moving beyond crypto-only trading to offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single unified platform. This expansion dramatically increases Coinbase's total addressable market — from the $2T crypto market to the $100T+ global securities market.

For Data Scientists, the equity story is about analytical leverage:

- **Cross-Asset Intelligence:** You are building models that span three asset classes, each with different volatility profiles, user behaviors, and regulatory constraints. The data science challenge is orders of magnitude more complex than single-asset analytics. Your RSU grant should reflect the analytical complexity and strategic value of cross-asset intelligence.
- **$2B Buyback Protection:** With $210K-$420K in RSUs, the buyback program provides meaningful protection for your equity. Coinbase is committing $2B to support COIN's price, giving your RSU grant a structural floor.
- **Revenue Attribution:** Your models directly impact Coinbase's revenue. User segmentation drives marketing efficiency, pricing experiments optimize take rates, and fraud detection protects margins. These contributions are measurable and directly tied to COIN's stock performance.
- **Negotiation Framing:** Position your RSU ask as "I'm building the analytical foundation for a $100T TAM expansion. My models will determine how Coinbase allocates resources across stocks, crypto, and prediction markets. My RSU grant should reflect the strategic value of this analytical work."
