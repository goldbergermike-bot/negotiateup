#### Negotiation DNA

The Technical Program Manager at Coinbase is the execution engine behind the "Everything Exchange" — the 2026 initiative to bring stocks, crypto, and prediction markets onto a single platform. At the TPM L4-L5 level, you orchestrate cross-functional delivery across engineering, product, design, compliance, and legal teams. The Everything Exchange is not a single launch — it is a coordinated sequence of regulatory approvals, technical milestones, and market entries that must be executed in precise order across multiple jurisdictions. Coinbase (NASDAQ: COIN) compensates TPMs with RSU grants on a 4-year vest, and the $2B share buyback program protects your equity. You are the person who ensures the Everything Exchange ships on time — and at Coinbase, launch timing directly impacts competitive positioning and revenue capture.

#### Everything Exchange — The $2B Buyback-Protected Equity

Coinbase's "Everything Exchange" is the company's most ambitious product expansion since its founding. In 2026, Coinbase is moving beyond crypto-only trading to offer stocks, crypto, and prediction markets on a single unified platform. This expansion dramatically increases Coinbase's total addressable market — from the $2T crypto market to the $100T+ global securities market.

For Technical Program Managers, the equity story is about execution leverage:

- **Launch Timing = Market Capture:** The Everything Exchange is a first-mover opportunity. If Coinbase launches multi-asset trading before competitors, it captures users and market share that compound over time. As the TPM, you control the program cadence that determines whether Coinbase is first or second. This timing leverage justifies premium RSU grants.
- **$2B Buyback Protection:** With $155K-$330K in RSUs, the buyback program provides downside protection for your equity. Coinbase's $2B share repurchase commitment signals that your RSU grant has institutional backing.
- **Cross-Functional Complexity:** You are orchestrating the most complex program in Coinbase's history — one that spans engineering, legal, compliance, and regulatory affairs across multiple jurisdictions. This is not a two-team program — it is an enterprise-scale initiative with dozens of dependencies. The compensation should reflect program complexity.
- **Negotiation Framing:** Position your ask as "I'm the TPM ensuring the Everything Exchange ships on schedule. A missed launch window costs Coinbase millions in lost first-mover advantage. My RSU grant should reflect the revenue impact of on-time delivery."
