**Negotiation DNA**

Solutions Architects at T-Mobile design the technical architecture for enterprise wireless solutions, 5G-enabled services, and internal platform systems. This role sits at the intersection of T-Mobile's 5G network capabilities and customer-facing solutions — translating enterprise requirements and consumer needs into technical designs that leverage T-Mobile's network advantage. Whether you're architecting enterprise 5G private network solutions, designing fixed wireless access (FWA) deployment architectures, or building internal platforms that support T-Mobile's operations, you shape how T-Mobile's 5G leadership translates into revenue.

T-Mobile's enterprise business is growing rapidly, driven by 5G solutions that competitors cannot match. Solutions Architects who can design compelling enterprise offerings that leverage T-Mobile's network advantage drive significant revenue growth.
