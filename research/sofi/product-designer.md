#### Negotiation DNA

Product Designers at SoFi shape the member experience across a full-service financial platform -- lending, banking, investing, and beyond -- serving 10M+ members. With SoFi achieving $1B quarterly revenue and projecting 30% growth, designers are directly accountable for conversion funnels, engagement loops, and cross-product adoption flows that drive member lifetime value. SoFi's publicly traded status (NASDAQ: SOFI, ~$15B+ market cap) means UX decisions have measurable impact on the metrics investors track.

The design challenge at SoFi is uniquely complex: designers must make sophisticated financial products feel simple and trustworthy while navigating regulatory disclosure requirements, compliance constraints, and the technical realities of banking infrastructure. The integrated platform model means every design decision must consider cross-product journeys -- how a banking experience flows into lending, how an investment dashboard connects to financial planning. This systemic design thinking is what makes SoFi designers LTV Accelerators.
