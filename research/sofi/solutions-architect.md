#### Negotiation DNA

Solutions Architects at SoFi bridge the gap between technical platform capabilities and business requirements across SoFi's integrated financial ecosystem -- lending, banking, investing, Galileo BaaS, and Technisys core banking. With $1B quarterly revenue and 30% growth outlook, Solutions Architects are critical to enabling enterprise client integrations on Galileo, architecting internal platform solutions that span multiple product lines, and ensuring SoFi's technology stack can scale with its aggressive growth targets.

SoFi's publicly traded status (NASDAQ: SOFI, ~$15B+ market cap) elevates the Solutions Architect role: every architectural recommendation influences build-vs-buy decisions, platform scalability, and the technical foundations that support 10M+ members and 130M+ Galileo accounts. Solutions Architects at SoFi are uniquely positioned at the intersection of consumer fintech, enterprise BaaS, and core banking modernization -- a breadth of scope that few fintech companies can offer.
