#### Negotiation DNA

Technical Program Managers at SoFi orchestrate the cross-functional execution of complex technical initiatives across SoFi's integrated financial platform -- spanning lending, banking, investing, Galileo BaaS, and Technisys core banking. With SoFi achieving $1B quarterly revenue and projecting 30% growth, TPMs are the execution backbone that ensures engineering, product, design, compliance, and operations teams deliver LTV-accelerating features on schedule and within regulatory constraints.

SoFi's publicly traded status (NASDAQ: SOFI, ~$15B+ market cap) adds urgency to program execution: features tied to quarterly earnings commitments must ship on time, platform migrations must execute without disruption, and enterprise client deployments must meet contractual timelines. TPMs who can navigate this intersection of technical complexity, regulatory compliance, and business urgency are essential to SoFi's growth trajectory.

TPMs who can integrate these regulatory workstreams into technical program timelines -- rather than treating compliance as a blocker -- ship products faster while maintaining the regulatory standing required of a chartered bank.

4. **Technisys Migration Execution:** The Technisys core banking platform modernization is one of SoFi's most complex multi-year technical programs. TPMs driving this migration manage the risk of platform transitions that affect every product line and 10M+ members. Flawless execution of this migration is worth billions in preserved member trust and platform capability.
