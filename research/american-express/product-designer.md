**Negotiation DNA**

Product Designers at AmEx create the digital experiences that embody the premium membership brand. Design quality directly impacts cardmember perception, digital engagement, and ultimately card fee revenue. AmEx has invested heavily in its design organization, recruiting from top tech companies and design agencies. The design bar is set by the premium brand promise.

AmEx's premium brand demands design quality that exceeds industry standards. Every digital touchpoint must reinforce the membership premium positioning. Designers who can deliver this premium-grade experience are rare and command above-market compensation.
