**Negotiation DNA**

In 2026, the highest-leverage senior role is on the Flink team: Confluent is investing heavily in making Flink the unified stream processing standard, and senior engineers building Flink capabilities are directly building Confluent's primary growth product. The balanced comp model means three levers. Confluent refreshes are typically competitive, but maximizing the initial grant is always priority #1. Bring competing offers from Databricks or Snowflake — Confluent responds well to data infrastructure competitors. [Source: Confluent Senior SWE Comp 2025-2026]
