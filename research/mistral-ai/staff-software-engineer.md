**Negotiation DNA**
Staff Software Engineers at Mistral AI are technical leaders who define the architecture of Europe's most important AI systems. At this level, you set the technical direction for entire domains — whether that's the distributed training framework that enables Mistral's mixture-of-experts models, the inference optimization stack that makes La Plateforme commercially viable, or the open-weight release pipeline that maintains Mistral's community credibility. Staff is where Mistral's pre-IPO equity becomes a serious wealth-creation vehicle: at $6B+ valuation with IPO trajectory, a €400K equity grant could be worth multiples if Mistral reaches a $20B+ public valuation. You are among the scarcest talent in European AI — demand accordingly.
