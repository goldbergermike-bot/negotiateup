**Negotiation DNA**: Staff Software Engineers at Fly.io are technical leaders defining the architecture of the edge application platform, making foundational decisions about microVM orchestration, global networking, and distributed systems that shape the company's technical future.

#### Lever 1: Platform Architecture — "I define the architecture of the entire edge application platform. At Fly.io's size, this is a company-defining role."
#### Lever 2: Technical Leadership — "As one of the most senior engineers, I set technical standards and mentor the team."
#### Lever 3: Startup CTO-Track — "Staff engineering at a startup is often a path to CTO/VP Eng. I expect compensation reflecting this trajectory."
#### Lever 4: Substantial Equity — "Staff-level startup equity should be a meaningful ownership stake."

> **Negotiate Up Strategy**: Open at $225,000 base with equity valued at $100K-$150K/year. Accept-at floor: $340,000 total comp. Negotiate for significant equity.
