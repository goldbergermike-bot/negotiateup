**Negotiation DNA**

Solutions Architects at Verizon design the technical architecture for enterprise networking solutions, 5G-enabled services, and customer-facing platforms. Verizon's enterprise business is a major revenue driver — the company serves thousands of enterprise and government customers with networking, security, IoT, and managed services. Solutions Architects translate enterprise requirements into technical designs that leverage Verizon's premium network infrastructure, 5G capabilities, and edge computing platform.

The role sits at the intersection of sales, engineering, and product — working directly with enterprise customers to design solutions while coordinating with internal engineering teams to ensure deliverability. This customer-facing technical role commands strong compensation because it requires a rare combination of deep architecture expertise and strong communication skills.
