PMs at Trellix navigate one of the most complex product strategy challenges in cybersecurity — integrating two major product portfolios while competing in the fast-growing XDR market against CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, and Microsoft. Under STG ownership, PMs balance platform integration with revenue growth.

"The XDR market is rapidly evolving. I bring the competitive understanding and product vision to position Trellix's platform against CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, and other XDR competitors."

"Deciding which McAfee Enterprise and FireEye capabilities to integrate, deprecate, or reimagine is a defining product strategy challenge. I bring experience making disciplined portfolio decisions."

"Existing McAfee Enterprise and FireEye customers must be smoothly transitioned to the unified Trellix platform. My customer migration experience ensures retention during this critical transition."

"STG expects strong growth. My product decisions directly impact revenue through platform pricing, feature prioritization, and customer expansion."
