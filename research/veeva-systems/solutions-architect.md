#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Veeva Systems

Solutions Architects at Veeva are trusted advisors to the world's largest pharmaceutical companies. You design implementation architectures for Vault, Data Cloud, and Commercial Cloud products that must satisfy both technical requirements and regulatory constraints. This requires understanding pharmaceutical workflows at a depth that general enterprise SAs never develop.

Veeva's land-and-expand business model depends on SAs who can identify expansion opportunities within existing pharmaceutical accounts. Your ability to architect solutions that demonstrate the value of additional Veeva products directly impacts revenue growth and customer retention.
