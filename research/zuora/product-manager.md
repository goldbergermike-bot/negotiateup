**Negotiation DNA**
Product Managers at Zuora own the strategy and execution for subscription billing and revenue management products. PMs here define the features that help companies launch, scale, and optimize their subscription businesses — pricing and packaging tools, billing automation, revenue recognition, subscription analytics, and AI-powered optimization features. The domain requires understanding both the technical complexity of billing systems and the business strategy of subscription monetization.

Zuora compensates PMs competitively for a company of its size, with packages that stretch to attract talent from larger enterprise software companies. At Zuora's scale, PMs have significantly broader product ownership than at larger companies — you might own the entire pricing and packaging module, not just a single feature within it. This scope creates both career growth opportunities and negotiation leverage.

Zuora's PM culture values deep customer empathy and domain expertise. PMs work closely with subscription businesses (from startups to Fortune 500) and need to understand the specific challenges of recurring revenue management. PMs who bring existing subscription economy knowledge accelerate product decisions and customer value delivery.

**Zuora Monetization Product Premium**
Product Managers who bring direct experience with subscription billing products, pricing strategy, or revenue management platforms have the strongest negotiating position. The subscription billing domain is complex — understanding proration rules, usage metering, multi-currency billing, and revenue recognition requires domain depth that general enterprise PMs may lack.

PMs with experience in the subscription economy — whether from other billing platforms, SaaS companies that have built internal billing systems, or consulting roles focused on subscription business models — bring directly transferable expertise.
