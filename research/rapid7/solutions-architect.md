#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Rapid7

Solutions Architects at Rapid7 work at the intersection of technical depth and customer engagement. They design Insight Platform deployments that integrate with complex enterprise environments — connecting InsightVM with patch management systems, feeding InsightIDR with diverse log sources, and orchestrating InsightConnect workflows across security tools.

The SA role directly enables enterprise deals by providing technical credibility and deployment confidence. Rapid7's multi-product platform creates opportunities for SAs to drive expansion across the product portfolio.
