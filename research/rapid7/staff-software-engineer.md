#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Rapid7

Staff Software Engineers at Rapid7 operate at the highest individual contributor levels, setting technical direction across the Insight Platform. They architect platform-wide solutions, drive engineering standards, and influence the technical roadmap for products serving thousands of enterprises. Rapid7's ~$4B market cap and unified platform strategy create significant scope for Staff-level impact.

At this level, Rapid7 competes with Staff roles at larger cybersecurity companies (CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks) and Big Tech. The breadth of the Insight Platform — spanning VM, SIEM, SOAR, cloud security, and AppSec — offers architectural challenges that match the scope of Staff roles at much larger companies.
