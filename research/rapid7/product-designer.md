#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Rapid7

Product Designers at Rapid7 solve the challenge of making enterprise security tools usable. The Insight Platform spans VM, SIEM, SOAR, and cloud security — each with distinct user workflows and data visualization requirements. Designers must create cohesive experiences across these products while handling the complexity of security data.

Rapid7's Boston office is in a competitive design market. The company invests in design as a product differentiator, recognizing that usability drives adoption and renewal in the enterprise security market.
