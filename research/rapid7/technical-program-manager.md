#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Rapid7

TPMs at Rapid7 drive the execution of cross-functional programs that span the Insight Platform's product portfolio. The unified platform strategy requires coordination across multiple engineering teams with independent product roadmaps. TPMs ensure these teams deliver cohesive platform capabilities on schedule while managing technical dependencies and risks.

Rapid7's ~$4B market cap and multi-product platform create significant scope for TPM impact — programs may span cloud infrastructure migration, cross-product feature integration, compliance certifications, and platform scalability initiatives.
