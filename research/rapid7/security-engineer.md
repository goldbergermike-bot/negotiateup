#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Rapid7

Security Engineers at Rapid7 carry the dual mandate of securing both the company's infrastructure and its products. As a public cybersecurity company, a security incident at Rapid7 would be reputationally devastating. This creates an environment where internal security engineering is existentially important.

Rapid7's own security team uses many of the company's products internally, creating a unique feedback loop. Security Engineers both protect the company and serve as expert users who inform product development.
