#### Negotiation DNA — Why This Role Commands a Premium at Rapid7

Product Managers at Rapid7 own product strategy for the Insight Platform's product suite. The PM role requires understanding both the security landscape (evolving threats, compliance requirements, SOC workflows) and enterprise buyer needs. Rapid7's ~$4B market cap and unified platform strategy create PMs with significant strategic scope.

PMs at Rapid7 work across InsightVM (vulnerability management), InsightIDR (cloud SIEM), InsightConnect (SOAR), InsightCloudSec (cloud security), and InsightAppSec (application security). The breadth of the portfolio and competitive market dynamics create a demanding and impactful PM role.
