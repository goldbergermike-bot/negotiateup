**Negotiation DNA**

Spendesk's 2026 Zero-Human Audit vision means Senior Engineers are designing and building the autonomous compliance agents that will define the next generation of European spend management.

The Compliance Sovereignty positioning makes this role exceptionally valuable. Spendesk is deliberately building a platform where European companies can manage spend under EU regulatory frameworks without relying on US-based infrastructure. Senior Engineers who can architect systems that maintain the 99.8% catch-rate while scaling across multiple EU jurisdictions are rare — and Spendesk knows it. The talent pool of engineers with both deep backend expertise and financial compliance understanding is shallow, giving experienced candidates meaningful leverage.

Senior Engineers who join now are building the system that will drive that liquidity event. The Paris headquarters creates a natural compensation anchor, but London and Berlin hires should expect adjusted packages that account for local market premiums.

### Zero-Human Audit — The Compliance Sovereignty Premium

**Lever 1 — Agentic Integrity System Design:** "Spendesk's Agentic Integrity vision requires Senior Engineers who can design autonomous compliance workflows, not just implement features. I'll be architecting the systems that deliver Zero-Human Audit at scale — where every transaction is validated, categorized, and flagged without manual intervention. My ask of €85,000 base reflects the fact that this work would command €100,000+ at a public fintech, and I'm accepting equity risk in exchange for upside."

As a Senior Engineer, I'll own subsystems where a regression could directly impact that number. I'm looking for €60,000 in options over four years to reflect the long-term value of the systems I'll be building and protecting."

Senior Engineers who can lead this work are not available on the open market at mid-band pricing. I'd like to discuss an options package closer to €65,000, recognizing this scarcity."

**Lever 4 — EU Expansion Engineering Premium:** "Every new EU market Spendesk enters — and I understand expansion is a priority — requires engineering work to adapt the platform to local tax codes, banking APIs, and compliance regimes.
