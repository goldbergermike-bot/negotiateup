**Negotiation DNA**

Product Managers at Disney own the strategy and roadmap for products that touch hundreds of millions of consumers across the world's most iconic entertainment portfolio. Whether you're driving Disney+ subscriber growth strategy, defining Parks guest experience technology, or building ESPN's next-generation digital sports platform, you sit at the intersection of technology, entertainment, and consumer experience at a $200B company. Disney PMs have unique leverage because product decisions don't just affect metrics — they shape how the world experiences Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, and Pixar.

Disney has significantly elevated the PM function, hiring product leaders from Google, Netflix, Amazon, and Apple. PMs at Disney have meaningful P&L ownership, direct access to senior creative and business leadership, and the opportunity to shape products that consumers are passionate about.

**Disney Product Strategy Premium**

Disney's PMs have a unique advantage: they manage products for brands that consumers love. This emotional connection means Disney+ product decisions get more user attention, Parks technology gets more testing through millions of daily interactions, and ESPN product launches generate passionate engagement. PMs who understand how to leverage this brand advantage while driving rigorous product metrics are exceptionally valuable.
