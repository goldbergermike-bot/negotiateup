**Negotiation DNA**

Solutions Architects at Pinterest bridge the gap between Pinterest's advertising and commerce platform and the merchants, retailers, and brands who drive revenue through it — designing technical integrations, building product catalog pipelines, and architecting commerce solutions that connect Pinterest's 500M+ MAU with the products they want to buy. Pinterest's SA role is uniquely strategic: unlike pure SaaS companies, Pinterest Solutions Architects must understand visual search technology, recommendation algorithms, and commerce APIs while translating them into merchant-facing solutions that drive advertiser spend and shopping revenue. The September 2026 AI Shopping Assistant launch dramatically expands the SA role — merchants will need to integrate their product catalogs with Pinterest's conversational AI, set up real-time inventory feeds for AI-powered product matching, and configure their Shopping Assistant presence to capture AI-driven purchase intent.
