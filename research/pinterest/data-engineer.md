**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at Pinterest build and maintain the data infrastructure that powers every product decision, ML model, and business metric across a platform serving 500M+ monthly active users. Pinterest generates petabytes of visual interaction data daily — Pin views, searches, saves, clicks, and commerce events — and data engineers build the pipelines, warehouses, and real-time streaming systems that make this data usable for recommendation engines, ad targeting systems, and commerce analytics. The September 2026 AI Shopping Assistant launch creates massive new data engineering requirements: real-time product catalog ingestion from thousands of merchants, user interaction event streams for conversational AI training, commerce transaction data pipelines for revenue attribution, and the data infrastructure backbone that every ML model powering the Shopping Assistant depends on.
