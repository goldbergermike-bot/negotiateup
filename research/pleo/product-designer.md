**Negotiation DNA**

Product Designers at Pleo shape the daily experience of finance teams, employees, and administrators across 30,000+ businesses in Europe. Unlike consumer design roles, designing for Pleo's spend management platform requires navigating complex financial workflows — expense submission, approval chains, budget management, card controls, and Cash-Management Yield dashboards — while maintaining the simplicity and delight that differentiates Pleo from legacy expense tools. Your design decisions directly influence adoption rates, feature engagement, and ultimately the transaction volume that powers Pleo's Real-Time Yield engine.

Pleo's Liquidity-Expansion strategy is deeply dependent on design excellence. The company's growth depends on making spend management so intuitive that businesses move more of their financial activity onto the platform, increasing the float available for yield generation. Every friction point you eliminate, every workflow you streamline, and every insight you surface through design directly contributes to platform stickiness and revenue growth. With 30,000+ merchants already on the platform, even small improvements in activation and engagement compound into significant financial impact.

As a pre-IPO company ($4.7B peak valuation, backed by Bain Capital, Thrive Capital, and Stripes), Pleo offers meaningful equity upside for designers who join before a potential public listing. Your negotiation should emphasize the revenue impact of design in a fintech context — where conversion rates, onboarding completion, and feature adoption are directly tied to transaction volume and Cash-Management Yield generation.

### Real-Time Yield — The Liquidity-Expansion Premium

**Lever 1 — Yield Experience Design:** "My design work on Pleo's financial dashboards, card management flows, and Cash-Management Yield interfaces directly influences how customers interact with their money on the platform. Better-designed yield visibility features increase customer confidence and retention, keeping more float on the platform. I'm requesting base compensation of DKK 710,000 / £85,000 / €80,000 to reflect this revenue-adjacent impact."

**Lever 2 — Pre-IPO Design Equity:** "Pleo's pre-IPO stage with backing from Bain Capital and Thrive Capital means design decisions made now will define the product experience that drives IPO valuation. I'd like an option grant of DKK 400,000 / £44,000 / €40,000 over four years, recognizing that the design system and product experience I build will compound in value through the public listing."

**Lever 3 — Liquidity-Expansion Activation Design:** "Pleo's Liquidity-Expansion depends on activating and retaining users across its 30,000+ merchant base. Every percentage point improvement in onboarding completion, card activation, and feature adoption that I drive through design translates directly to increased transaction volume. I'd like a performance bonus tied to activation and engagement metrics that reflect my Liquidity-Expansion contribution."

**Lever 4 — European Multi-Market Design Complexity:** "Designing spend management experiences for multiple European markets requires understanding diverse regulatory requirements, cultural expectations, and business practices. My ability to create cohesive cross-market experiences while respecting local needs is a specialized skill that accelerates Pleo's European expansion. I'm requesting a signing bonus of DKK 65,000 / £7,500 / €7,000 and a Real-Time Yield design impact bonus."
