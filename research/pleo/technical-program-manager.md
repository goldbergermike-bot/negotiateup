**Negotiation DNA**

Technical Program Managers at Pleo orchestrate the cross-functional delivery of the most complex and high-impact initiatives on the platform. From multi-quarter migrations of the payment processing stack to coordinated launches of new financial products across European markets, you are the connective tissue that ensures engineering, product, design, compliance, and operations execute in concert. Your programs directly determine the pace at which Pleo can expand its Cash-Management Yield capabilities and execute its Liquidity-Expansion strategy across 30,000+ merchants.

At a company like Pleo — where real money flows through the platform in real time — program execution carries financial risk. A delayed launch means lost yield revenue. A poorly coordinated migration can disrupt card services for thousands of businesses. A compliance gap can trigger regulatory action across multiple European jurisdictions. TPMs at Pleo must combine deep technical understanding with exceptional stakeholder management and a fintech-specific appreciation for the financial consequences of execution failures.

Pleo's pre-IPO trajectory ($4.7B peak valuation, backed by Bain Capital, Thrive Capital, and Stripes) makes TPMs especially valuable — the company needs disciplined program execution to deliver the product roadmap that justifies its valuation. Your negotiation should emphasize the revenue impact of program velocity and the risk mitigation you provide for Real-Time Yield infrastructure programs.
