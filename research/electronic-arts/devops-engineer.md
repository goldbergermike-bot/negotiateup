**Negotiation DNA**

DevOps Engineers at EA maintain the infrastructure and CI/CD pipelines that enable 10,000+ developers across 20+ studios to ship games and live-service updates on tight cadences. EA's infrastructure spans multiple cloud providers (AWS, GCP), on-premise data centers for low-latency game servers, and a complex multi-studio build system that compiles game binaries measured in hundreds of gigabytes. The live-service model demands zero-downtime deployments, automated rollbacks, and real-time monitoring across millions of concurrent players. EA's DevOps function has grown in strategic importance as the company shifted from annual ship cycles to continuous delivery for live-service titles. [Source: EA Platform Engineering 2025]
