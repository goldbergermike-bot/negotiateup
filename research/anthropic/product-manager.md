**Negotiation DNA**
Product Managers at Anthropic define what Claude becomes — shaping the product strategy for one of the most widely used AI systems in the world. This is AI-native product management at its most consequential: PMs here don't just optimize funnels or prioritize backlogs — they make decisions about capability deployment, safety trade-offs, and user experience that directly impact how millions of people interact with frontier AI. Anthropic's Constitutional AI philosophy means product decisions are always filtered through a safety lens, making this role uniquely different from PM work at any other tech company. You'll work at the intersection of cutting-edge research (what Claude can do) and responsible deployment (what Claude should do), collaborating directly with researchers, engineers, and policy teams.

**The Safety Premium — $300K+ Base Benchmark**
Anthropic pays the highest base salaries in the AI industry — the "Safety Premium." This isn't charity; it's strategic. Anthropic attracts mission-driven talent who want to build AI safely, and the high base ensures they don't lose candidates to OpenAI's equity-heavy offers. Use Anthropic's base as your benchmark: "Anthropic is offering me $240K-$305K base for comparable work. Your base needs to match or the equity must compensate for the gap." At the senior PM level, Anthropic's base pushes past $300K, establishing the highest PM base in the AI industry. The Safety Premium also provides financial stability during the pre-IPO period — you're not dependent on a liquidity event to earn market-rate compensation. When comparing offers, Anthropic's $240K+ PM base is the floor, not the ceiling.
