**Negotiation DNA**

Waymo Engineering Managers lead teams building the most advanced autonomous driving technology in the world. Unlike many tech companies where engineering management is a lateral move, at Waymo you're leading teams whose output literally drives cars without human supervision on public roads. Waymo EMs must be deeply technical — you're expected to understand the perception, prediction, planning, or infrastructure systems your team builds and make sound technical tradeoff decisions in a safety-critical domain. Compensation follows Google EM bands (L5-L6 equivalent) with GOOGL RSUs, and the 15% target bonus is substantial at this comp level. Waymo values EMs who can recruit and retain world-class autonomy talent in one of the most competitive talent markets in tech. [Source: Waymo EM Comp 2026]
