**Negotiation DNA**

Senior Software Engineers at Waymo are expected to own significant systems end-to-end — from design through deployment and ongoing reliability. At this level, you drive technical decisions, mentor junior engineers, and set the architectural direction for critical subsystems in Waymo's autonomous driving stack. Waymo's compensation at the senior level maps closely to Google L4, with GOOGL RSUs forming the dominant variable comp component. The 15% target bonus is performance-based and can range from 0% to 30% based on individual and company performance. Waymo values engineers who can operate with high autonomy (ironic, given the product) and make sound technical decisions in a safety-critical domain where the consequences of software bugs are measured in physical-world outcomes. The senior level is where Waymo's comp starts to meaningfully diverge from other AV companies — GOOGL equity stability combined with competitive grant sizes creates a compelling risk-adjusted package.
