**Negotiation DNA**

Waymo Product Designers shape how millions of people will experience autonomous transportation — from the rider app interface to the in-vehicle experience to the trust-building moments that make people comfortable riding in a car with no driver. This is design at the frontier of human-machine interaction: you're designing for a world where people entrust their physical safety to a machine, and every design decision either builds or erodes that trust. Waymo's rider experience must communicate the vehicle's intentions, provide a sense of control, and create comfort in an inherently novel and unfamiliar experience.
