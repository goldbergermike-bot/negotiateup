**No-Cliff Day-1 Liquidity Strategy**

Workday RSUs vest quarterly with NO cliff from Day 1. Your first paycheck includes RSU value. At a FAANG company with a 1-year cliff, you see $0 in equity for 12 months. A Workday RSU grant of $200K with Day-1 vesting delivers $12.5K in real liquidity in Q1 — while a $240K grant at a cliff company delivers $0 for the first year. When comparing offers, calculate the "liquidity-adjusted" value: Workday's grant delivers faster real-world value.

A Workday RSU grant of $160K with Day-1 quarterly vesting is worth more in real liquidity than a $192K grant at a cliff company where you see nothing for 12 months. Use this framing explicitly: "I'm comparing your offer against a company with a higher RSU total, but their 1-year cliff means I see nothing for 12 months. Workday's Day-1 vesting is worth real money to me — I'd accept a slightly lower total if we can push the base to [$X]."
