**No-Cliff Day-1 Liquidity Strategy**

Workday RSUs vest quarterly with NO cliff from Day 1. Your first paycheck includes RSU value. At a FAANG company with a 1-year cliff, you see $0 in equity for 12 months. A Workday RSU grant of $250K with Day-1 vesting delivers $15.6K in real liquidity in Q1 — while a $300K grant at a cliff company delivers $0 for the first year. When comparing offers, calculate the "liquidity-adjusted" value: Workday's grant delivers faster real-world value.

Data scientists are among the most aggressively recruited roles in tech, meaning you likely have multiple competing offers. A Workday RSU grant of $230K with Day-1 quarterly vesting is worth more in real liquidity than a $276K grant at a cliff company where you see nothing for 12 months. Use the Day-1 vesting as a tiebreaker when offers look similar on paper: "Workday pays me from Day 1. The other company makes me wait a year."
