**No-Cliff Day-1 Liquidity Strategy**

Workday RSUs vest quarterly with NO cliff from Day 1. Your first paycheck includes RSU value. At a FAANG company with a 1-year cliff, you see $0 in equity for 12 months. A Workday RSU grant of $240K with Day-1 vesting delivers $15K in real liquidity in Q1 — while a $288K grant at a cliff company delivers $0 for the first year. When comparing offers, calculate the "liquidity-adjusted" value: Workday's grant delivers faster real-world value.

For PMs evaluating offers from Google, Meta, or Amazon, the cliff dynamic is especially painful — PM hiring cycles are long, and waiting another 12 months post-start for any equity to vest means potentially 18+ months from first interview to first share. A Workday RSU grant of $220K with Day-1 quarterly vesting is worth more in real liquidity than a $264K grant at a cliff company where you see nothing for 12 months.
