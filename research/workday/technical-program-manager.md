**No-Cliff Day-1 Liquidity Strategy**

Workday RSUs vest quarterly with NO cliff from Day 1. Your first paycheck includes RSU value. At a FAANG company with a 1-year cliff, you see $0 in equity for 12 months. A Workday RSU grant of $210K with Day-1 vesting delivers $13.1K in real liquidity in Q1 — while a $252K grant at a cliff company delivers $0 for the first year. When comparing offers, calculate the "liquidity-adjusted" value: Workday's grant delivers faster real-world value.

TPMs frequently compare offers across Google, Amazon, Meta, and enterprise companies. A Workday RSU grant of $200K with Day-1 quarterly vesting is worth more in real liquidity than a $240K grant at a cliff company where you see nothing for 12 months. In Year 1, Workday delivers $50K in vested equity vs. $0 at a cliff competitor. For TPMs carrying program accountability from Day 1, getting equity from Day 1 aligns incentives correctly.
