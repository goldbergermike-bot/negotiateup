**No-Cliff Day-1 Liquidity Strategy**

Workday RSUs vest quarterly with NO cliff from Day 1. Your first paycheck includes RSU value. At a FAANG company with a 1-year cliff, you see $0 in equity for 12 months. A Workday RSU grant of $480K with Day-1 vesting delivers $30K in real liquidity in Q1 — while a $576K grant at a cliff company delivers $0 for the first year. When comparing offers, calculate the "liquidity-adjusted" value: Workday's grant delivers faster real-world value.

At the Staff level, the Day-1 vesting advantage is enormous. A Workday RSU grant of $440K with Day-1 quarterly vesting is worth more in real liquidity than a $528K grant at a cliff company where you see nothing for 12 months. In Year 1, Workday delivers $110K in vested equity across four quarters while the cliff competitor delivers $0. For a Staff engineer evaluating competing offers, this is $110K in real liquidity vs. $0 — a difference no percentage adjustment can erase.
