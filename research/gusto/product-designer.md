### AI-Powered UX Design Lever
Gusto is redesigning its core payroll experience around AI-generated recommendations — the system now suggests optimal pay schedules, flags potential compliance issues before they occur, and auto-generates tax documents. All of this requires thoughtful UX that builds trust in AI-driven financial decisions. Gusto's Head of Design has described this as "the biggest design challenge in our history" — making SMB owners comfortable delegating payroll decisions to AI.

Designers who understand trust design patterns, progressive disclosure for complex information, and AI transparency UX are in critical demand. The intersection of fintech UX and AI interaction design is an extremely niche skillset, and Gusto is willing to pay a premium for it.
