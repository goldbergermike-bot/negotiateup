### Platform Architecture Modernization Lever
Gusto's multi-year platform modernization — decomposing the Ruby on Rails monolith into domain-driven microservices — is the defining technical challenge for staff engineers in 2026. The initiative directly enables Gusto Embedded (which requires clean API boundaries) and international expansion (which requires jurisdiction-isolated services). Gusto's VP of Engineering has stated that completing the platform migration is a prerequisite for IPO readiness.

Staff engineers leading this effort have outsized organizational influence and comp leverage. The migration touches every engineering team, making the staff engineer role both cross-cutting and strategically visible to leadership. If you have experience leading similar monolith-to-microservices transformations at fintech companies, you are essentially irreplaceable during this transition period.
