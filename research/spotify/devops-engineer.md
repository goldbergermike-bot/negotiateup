**Negotiation DNA**

Spotify's infrastructure serves 640M+ monthly active users streaming audio in real-time across 180+ markets — one of the highest-scale, lowest-latency distributed systems in tech. DevOps Engineers at Spotify build and maintain the global audio delivery infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, container orchestration (Spotify was an early Kubernetes adopter), observability platforms, and the internal developer platform (Backstage — which Spotify open-sourced and is now used by thousands of companies). The Backstage connection is a unique negotiation lever: Spotify created the industry-standard internal developer portal, and the DevOps team that maintains it has outsized visibility and impact. The 10% bonus is standard; equity is the primary lever. Spotify's GCP-based infrastructure (after migrating from on-prem) means cloud-native DevOps skills are highly valued.
