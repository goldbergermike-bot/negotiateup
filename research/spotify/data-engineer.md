**Negotiation DNA**

Data Engineers at Spotify build the data infrastructure that powers every data-driven decision across the company — from the recommendation engine (Discover Weekly, Release Radar, AI DJ) to ad measurement (attribution, incrementality) to creator analytics (Spotify for Artists) to financial reporting ($11B+ in royalty calculations). Spotify processes billions of audio events per day across 640M+ users, making this one of the highest-volume real-time data engineering challenges in tech. Spotify's data stack is heavily GCP-based (BigQuery, Dataflow, Pub/Sub) with custom tooling built on top. The company was an early adopter of event-driven architectures and data mesh principles. Key domains include: audio analytics pipelines (listening events at 640M+ user scale), recommendation data infrastructure (feature stores for ML models), ad measurement pipelines (real-time attribution and campaign analytics), and royalty calculation systems (accurate payment to millions of creators). The 10% bonus is standard; equity is the primary lever. [Source: Spotify Data Eng Comp 2025-2026]
